1. Open the [Scaleway website](https://www.scaleway.com/en/) in a web browser.
2. Click **Sign Up** in the upper right corner of the page. You are redirected to the account creation page.
3. Select an account type (**Personal** or **Professional**) and fill out your first and last name, as well as your email address.
4. Check the box to agree to Scaleway's [Terms and Services](https://www.scaleway.com/en/contracts/), and Scaleway's [Data Protection Agreement](https://www.scaleway.com/en/contracts/).
5. Check the box and solve the [Friendly Captcha](https://friendlycaptcha.com/). Then, click **Create account**. A message displays to request that you check your email inbox for the verification email.
6. Click the verification link in your mailbox to confirm your email address and agree to our terms of service. You are redirected to the **Add billing information** page.
7. Fill out the required fields, then click **Add billing address**. The **Add a credit card** page displays.
    <Message type="important">
      You can click **Add later** to [enter your billing information at a later time](/console/billing/how-to/add-payment-method/), and directly access your Organization dashboard in the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/organization), but you cannot use Scaleway products without billing information. 
    </Message>
8. Enter your credit card information, then click **Add credit card**. A [3D Secure](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/3-D_Secure) validation is initiated. 
    <Message type="note"> 
    Depending on your bank, you may be asked to enter a code received by SMS or to confirm via an application on your smartphone.
    </Message>

Once your credit card is validated, you are taken to your Organization dashboard in the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/organization).
