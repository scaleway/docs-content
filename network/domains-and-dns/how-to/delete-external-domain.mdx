<Macro id="iam-requirements" />
 
<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [linked your domain](/network/domains-and-dns/how-to/add-external-domain) with Scaleway Domains and DNS
</Message>

1. Click **Domains and DNS** in the **Network** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com) side menu. The Domains and DNS dashboard displays.
2. Click the domain you want to delete.
3. Click **Terminate management**. A pop-up displays.
4. Type **TERMINATE** to confirm the deletion of the domain, then click **Terminate domain management**.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-dns-delete-domain.webp" alt="" />
    <Message type="important">
      This will permanently delete your external domain and its related zones from Scaleway Domains and DNS. This action is irreversible and your domain name may become unresolvable.
    </Message>

<Message type="tip">
  You can also delete your domain from the list of domains by clicking <Icon name="more" />, then **Delete**.
</Message>
