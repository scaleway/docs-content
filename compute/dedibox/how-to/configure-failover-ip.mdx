<Message type="requirement">

  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com) 
  - You have performed the [linking process](/compute/dedibox/how-to/link-dedibox-account/) between Dedibox and Scaleway
  - You have [ordererd](/compute/dedibox/how-to/order-dedibox) a Dedibox server
  - You have [ordered](/compute/dedibox/how-to/order-failover-ip/) a failover IP

</Message>

The following pages provide information how to configure a [failover IP](/dedibox-network/ip-failover/concepts/#failover-ips) on Dedibox servers running various operating systems:

* [CentOS](/dedibox-network/ip-failover/how-to/configure-centos/)
* [Debian and Ubuntu Linux](/dedibox-network/ip-failover/how-to/configure-debian-ubuntu/)
* [FreeBSD](/dedibox-network/ip-failover/how-to/configure-freebsd/)
* [Windows Server](/dedibox-network/ip-failover/how-to/configure-windows/)

Further information about failover IPs refer to our dedicated [failover IP documentation](/dedibox-network/ip-failover/quickstart/).
