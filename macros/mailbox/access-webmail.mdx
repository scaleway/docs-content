<Message type="tip">
    The website to access your Mailbox is **[mailbox.scw.eu](https://mailbox.scw.eu/)**.
</Message>

1. In the **Email accounts** tab, click **[Access Webmail](https://mailbox.scw.eu/)** to open the email service application in a new browser tab.
2. Enter your full email address and password to access your Mailbox.
