This page shows how to reinstall your [Mac mini M1](/compute/apple-silicon/concepts#mac-mini-m1) from the Scaleway Console. This is useful if your Mac mini M1 is not reachable or you want to start from scratch on the machine.

<Message type="requirement">

  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [created a Mac mini M1](/compute/apple-silicon/how-to/create-mac-mini-m1/)

</Message>

1. Click **Apple silicon** in the **Compute** section of the side menu. A list of your Mac mini M1s displays.

  <Lightbox src="scaleway-m1-list.png" alt="" />

2. Click the Mac mini M1 you want to reinstall. Your Mac mini M1 **Overview** displays.

3. Scroll down to the **Reinstall Mac mini M1** section, and click **Reinstall**.

  <Lightbox src="scaleway-m1-reinstall.png" alt="" />

4. Click **Reinstall** to confirm.

    <Message type="important">

    When reinstalling your server all the data on the disk is deleted and all configuration is reset to the default configuration values of the image.

    </Message>
