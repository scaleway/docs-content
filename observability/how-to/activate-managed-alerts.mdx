This page shows you how to activate [managed alerts](/observability/cockpit/concepts/#managed-alerts) that will notify you on your products' behavior.


<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com/)
  - You have [activated your Cockpit](/observability/cockpit/how-to/activate-cockpit/)
</Message>

1. Log into your [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com/) account.

2. Click **Cockpit** in the side menu. The **Cockpit** overview page displays.

3. Click the **Alerts** tab. The **Alerts** page displays.

4. Click **Enable** to activate managed alerts.

5. Click **Add an email** to configure your [contact points](lien concept).

6. Enter the email address you wish to add to your contact points when prompted.

7. Click **Add email address**. The email address you have added displays in the list of your contact points.
