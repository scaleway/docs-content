Starting Monday 2nd September 2024, a new pricing will apply to the following TLDs when buying, renewing, and  transferring domains: `.beauty`, `.college`, `.hair`, `.makeup`, `.monster`, `.quest`, `.rent`, and `.skin`.

From Monday 9th September 2024, the pricing for `.com` and `.net` TLDs will also change.

Refer to the [dedicated page](https://www.scaleway.com/en/domains-name/) to consult our new pricing.
