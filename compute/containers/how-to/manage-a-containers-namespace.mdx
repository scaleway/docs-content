This page shows you how to manage your Scaleway Elements Containers Namespace. [Namespaces](/network/dns-cloud/concepts/#dns-namespace) allow you to group your containers and share environment variables between them.

<Message type="requirement">

  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [created a Containers namespace](/compute/containers/how-to/create-a-containers-namespace/)

</Message>

1. Click **Containers** in the **Compute** section of the side menu. The Containers page displays.

2. Click the Containers namespace you want to manage. 

3. Click the **Namespace settings** tab. Here you can view information about the region, registry endpoint and description of your namespace.

    <Lightbox src="scaleway-manage-cont-namespace.png" alt="" />

4. Add any additional environment variables you wish to add, by clicking **+Add**, entering the key/value pair, and clicking **Save**.
