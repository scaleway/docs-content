This page shows you how to activate [managed alerts](/observability/cockpit/concepts/#managed-alerts) which notifies you of your products' behavior.


<Message type="requirement">

  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/)
  - You have [activated your Cockpit](/observability/cockpit/how-to/activate-cockpit/)

</Message>


1. Click **Cockpit** in the Observability section of the [console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. The **Cockpit** overview page displays.

2. Click the **Alerts** tab. The **Alerts** page displays.

3. Click **Enable** to activate managed alerts.

4. Click **Add an email** to configure your [contact points](/observability/cockpit/concepts/#contact-points).

5. Enter the email address you wish to add to your contact points when prompted.

6. Click **Add email address**. The email address you have added displays in the list of your contact points.
