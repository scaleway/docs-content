This page shows how to create a Scaleway Webhosting. Webhosting is a managed service, designed for personal, business and everything in between. It allows you to host your website and databases and create personalized email addresses using your domain name.


<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [registered a domain name](/network/domains-and-dns/how-to/register-internal-domain/) at Scaleway or another registrar
</Message>

<Message type="tip">
  This content is also available as a [video](/compute/instances/videos/#create-your-first-instance)
</Message>

## How to create an Instance

1. Click **Webhosting** in the **Managed Services** section of the side menu. The Webhosting splash page displays.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-webhosting-splash.webp" alt="" />

    <Message type="note">
      If you have existing Webhostings in your account, you will see a list of
      these Webhostings. Click the <Icon name="plus" />
      button to create additional Webhostings.
    </Message>
2. Click **Order Webhosting**. The Webhosting creation wizard displays.
3. Complete the following steps in the wizard: 
    - Enter a **domain name** that you would link to your webhosting account. 
      <Message type="important">
        The domain must be registered already and you have to the registrant for this domain. If you do not have a domain name yet, you can [registered a domain name](/network/domains-and-dns/how-to/register-internal-domain/) before continuing.
      </Message>
    - Choose a **hosting plan**. Choose the offer that corresponds to the requirements for your webhosting. 
    - Choose wether you want to enable the **backup option** or not. 
      <Message type="tip">
        Activate this option to benefit from a daily automated backup of your webhosting account, accessible by FTP. The offer includes a daily backup of your website, emails and databases, retention of these backups for three days, and retention of a full weekly backup of your website for two months.
      </Message>
    - Enter your **email address**. We will send your cPanel password to the email address you enter in the form.
4. Click **Validate Webhosting** to finish. The creation of your Webhosting begins and you are redirected to the **Overview** tab. From here you can see information about your webhosting account, such as the link to the cPanel control panel, your webmail interface etc.

<Lightbox src="scaleway-instance-overview.webp" alt="" />

Proceed with the configuration of the DNS for your webhosting.
