This page shows you how to delete an Instance.

<Message type="important">

  Deleting an Instance is an irreversible action. Any data stored on your Instance will be lost.

</Message>

<Message type="requirement">

  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [created an Instance](/compute/instances/how-to/create-an-instance)
  
</Message>

## How to delete an Instance

1. Click **Instances** in the **Compute** section of the side menu. The Instances page displays.

2. Click the <Icon name="more" /> icon next to the Instance you want to delete, and select **Delete** from the drop-down menu.

  <Lightbox src="scaleway-delete-instance.webp" alt="" />

    A pop-up asks you to confirm the action.

3. Type **DELETE** and then click **Delete this Instance**

You are returned to the list of your Instances, where the Instance you deleted no longer appears.
