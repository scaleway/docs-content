[Custom models](https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/managed-inference/reference-content/supported-models/#custom-models) can now be deployed on Managed Inference.  
[Try it now](https://console.scaleway.com/inference/models/import) by providing a Hugging Face URL from a [compatible model](https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/managed-inference/reference-content/supported-models/#custom-models).
