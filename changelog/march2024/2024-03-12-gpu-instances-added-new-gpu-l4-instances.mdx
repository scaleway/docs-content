We are excited to introduce our latest GPU Instances based on [Nvidia L4](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/data-center/l4/) in the Paris (PAR2) and the Warsaw (WAW2) regions, a versatile and cost-effective GPU Instance.

Key features include:
- Nvidia L4 24GB (Ada Lovelace architecture)
- 4th generation Tensor cores
- 4rd Generation RT cores (Graphics capability)
- Available in multi-gpu 1, 2, 4 or 8 GPUs
