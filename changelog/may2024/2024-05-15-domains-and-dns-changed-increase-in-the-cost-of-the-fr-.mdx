As recently announced by AFNIC, the price of the .FR extension registration fee is about to increase by 11.1% effective from March 1st, 2024. You can find further details on this price adjustment [here](https://www.afnic.fr/observatoire-ressources/actualites/le-fr-passe-de-4-56e-a-507e-a-partir-du-1er-mars-2024/).

This price adjustment will subsequently influence the pricing of .FR domains purchased through Scaleway, commencing June 1st, 2024.
