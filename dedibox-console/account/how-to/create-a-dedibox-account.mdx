Follow these steps to get started with Scaleway Dedibox:

1. Go to the [Scaleway Dedibox](https://www.scaleway.com/en/dedibox/) website using your web browser.
2. Locate and click the **Create Dedibox Account** button situated at the top right corner of the website.
3. Fill in all the required information, review and accept the terms of service, and then proceed by clicking **Create my account**. A verification link will be sent to the email address you provided during registration.
4. Access your email inbox and click on the verification link to activate your account. This will direct you to the [Dedibox console](https://console.online.net) login screen.
5. Enter the username and password you selected during registration, then click **Sign In**.
   You are now connected to the Scaleway Dedibox console, and you can proceed to [order your first dedicated server](/dedibox/dedicated-servers/how-to/order-a-server/).
