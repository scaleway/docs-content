IPAM acts as a single source of truth for the IP addresses of Scaleway resources, offering a variety of functionalities to help you manage your Scaleway IPs efficiently. Whether you're tracking all private VPC IPs or organizing your IP address space, IPAM is designed to simplify your workflow and enhance your productivity.
