---
title: I cannot send emails from my Apple Silicon M1 as-a-Service
description: This page explains why you cannot send emails from Apple Silicon M1 as-a-Service
image: /assets/images/docs/scaleway.png
dates:
  validation: 2021-05-26
  posted: 2021-05-26
---

For security reasons the SMTP ports on Apple Silicon M1 as-a-Service machines are blocked. It is therefore not possible to send emails from your Mac mini M1.
