  ## Cockpit

  ### Cockpit supported endpoints

    The following [Cockpit](/cockpit/) endpoints are supported and will be logged in Audit Trail:

    - CreateExporter (`POST/cockpit/v1/regions/{region}/exporters`)
    - DeleteExporter (`DELETE/cockpit/v1/regions/{region}/exporters/{exporter_id}`)
    - UpdateExporter (`PATCH/cockpit/v1/regions/{region}/exporters/{exporter_id}`)
    - CreateDataSource (`POST/cockpit/v1/regions/{region}/data-sources`)
    - DeleteDataSource (`DELETE/cockpit/v1/regions/{region}/data-sources/{data_source_id}`)
    - UpdateDataSource (`PATCH/cockpit/v1/regions/{region}/data-sources/{data_source_id}`)
    - CreateToken (`POST/cockpit/v1/regions/{region}/tokens`)
    - DeleteToken (`DELETE/cockpit/v1/regions/{region}/tokens/{token_id}`)
    - EnableAlertManager (`POST/cockpit/v1/regions/{region}/alert-manager/enable`)
    - DisableAlertManager (`POST/cockpit/v1/regions/{region}/alert-manager/disable`)
    - CreateContactPoint (`POST/cockpit/v1/regions/{region}/alert-manager/contact-points`)
    - UpdateContactPoint (`PATCH/cockpit/v1/regions/{region}/alert-manager/contact-points`)
    - DeleteContactPoint (`POST/cockpit/v1/regions/{region}/alert-manager/contact-points/delete`)
    - EnableManagedAlerts (`POST/cockpit/v1/regions/{region}/alert-manager/managed-alerts/enable`)
    - DisableManagedAlerts (`POST/cockpit/v1/regions/{region}/alert-manager/managed-alerts/disable`)
    - EnableAlertRules (`POST/cockpit/v1/regions/{region}/alert-manager/enable-alert-rules`)
    - DisableAlertRules (`POST/cockpit/v1/regions/{region}/alert-manager/disable-alert-rules`)

</Accordion>
