Scaleway Data Warehouse for ClickHouse速 is fully integrated into [Cockpit](/cockpit/quickstart/), and allows you to quickly access your logs and metrics. This page explains how to get started with Scaleway Cockpit to monitor your deployments.

<Requirements />

- A Scaleway account logged into the [console](https://console.scaleway.com)
- [Owner](/iam/concepts/#owner) status or [IAM permissions](/iam/concepts/#permission) allowing you to perform actions in the intended Organization
- [Created a Data Warehouse deployment](/data-warehouse/how-to/create-deployment/)

## How to access the Grafana dashboard

1. From the Scaleway console [Cockpit overview page](https://console.scaleway.com/cockpit/overview), click **Access Grafana** in the top right corner.

2. From the **Scaleway Dashboards** panel, select:
    - **Data Warehouse for ClickHouse速** to access the logs and metrics of your deployments.

  You are directed to the selected dashboard. You can use the filters at the top of the page to refine the displayed information.

<Message type="note">
  You can also access the logs and metrics from the [console](https://console.scaleway.com) via the **Logging** and **Metrics** tabs of a deployment.
</Message>

## Available metrics

The following metrics are available to monitor your Data Warehouse deployments:

- Number of provisioned CPUs
- Amount of provisioned memory (RAM)
- Number of connections to the client
- Object Storage usage
- Block Storage usage
- Disk read and write throughput
