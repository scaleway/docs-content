You can choose the color scheme of the [Scaleway console](/account/concepts/#console) according to your preferences. We offer a light, dark, darker, or system-based color scheme.

<Requirements />

- A Scaleway account logged into the [console](https://console.scaleway.com)
- [Owner](/iam/concepts/#owner) status or [IAM permissions](/iam/concepts/#permission) allowing you to perform actions in the intended Organization

1. Access the [Settings space](https://console.scaleway.com/settings/) of your console.
2. Click [Theme](https://console.scaleway.com/settings/account/profile) under the **Account** section of the side menu.
3. Choose your preferred color scheme from the four available options:
    * Light
    * Dark
    * Darker
    * System

<Message type="note">
  Your selection is automatically saved once you have chosen a color scheme.
</Message>
