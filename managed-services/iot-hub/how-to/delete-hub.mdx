In case you do not need your IoT Hub any longer, you can remove it from your account via the Scaleway console. 

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [created an IoT Hub](/managed-services/iot-hub/how-to/create-hub)
</Message>

1. Click **IoT Hub** in the **Managed Services** section of the side menu. The list of your IoT Hubs displays.
2. Click the name of the IoT Hub you want to delete. The hub's overview page displays. 
3. Click **Delete Hub** to remove the selected hub from your account.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-iothub_delete.webp" alt="" />
    <Message type="important">
      Note delting your hub will also delete any device added to the Hub.
    </Message>
4. Type `DELETE` in the pop-up window and click **Delete Hub**.
    <Message type="tip">
      It is possible to delete multiple Hubs at once by selecting them in the Hub list.
    </Message>
