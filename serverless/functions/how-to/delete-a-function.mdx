This page shows you how to delete a function

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="important">
  This action will permanently delete your function. This action is irreversible and all data will be destroyed.
</Message>

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [created a function](/serverless/functions/how-to/create-a-function/)
</Message>

1. Click **Functions** in the **Serverless** section of the side menu. The functions page displays.
2. Click the relevant functions namespace.
2. Click the <Icon name="more" /> icon next to the function you want to delete, and select *Delete** from the drop down menu. 
3. Type **DELETE** in the pop-up that asks you to confirm the action, then click **Delete this function**.
