Starting November 1st 2024, [Edge Services](https://www.scaleway.com/en/edge-services/) is in General Availability for both Object Storage and Load Balancer usage.
This means that the product is now ready for production environments, and Edge Services is no longer free to use. Find out about the new [Edge Services pricing](https://www.scaleway.com/en/pricing/network/#edge-services) for full details.
