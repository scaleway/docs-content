<Message type="requirement">

- You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com/) 

</Message>


1. Click **Instances** in the **Compute** section of the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com) side menu. The Instances section displays.  
2. Click **Volumes** to display a list of all your current volumes. 
  <Lightbox src="scaleway-volumes-list.png" alt="" />
3. Click **Create a Volume** to create a volume that can be attached to any of your Instances.
  <Lightbox src="scaleway-create-volume.png" alt="" />
4. Choose a region for the volume.
  <Message type="important">

  The volume must be in the same region as the Instance you want to connect it to.

  </Message>

  <Lightbox src="scaleway-choose-region.png" alt="" />
5. Select Block Storage when configuring the volume. Enter the volume name and select a size.
  <Lightbox src="scaleway-configure-block-volume.png" alt="" />
6. Click **Create a new volume** to confirm the creation of the volume.
