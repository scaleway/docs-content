<Macro id="requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/)
  - You have [created](/storage/block/how-to/create-a-volume/) a Block Storage volume
</Message>

1. Click **Block Storage** in the **Storage** section of the Scaleway Console side menu. The Block Storage page displays.
2. Click <Icon name="more" /> > **Increase size** next to the volume of your choice. A pop-up displays.
3. Increase the size according to your preference with the <Icon name="plus" /> button.
4. Click **Update volume size** to confirm.
    <Message type="note">
      The maximum size of a Block volume is 10 TB.
    </Message>

    <Message type="tip">
      Refer to our [dedicated documentation](/storage/block/api-cli/managing-a-volume/#increasing-the-partition-size-of-the-volume) to find out how to increase the partition size of your volume.
    </Message>
