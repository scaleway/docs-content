title: Kubernetes version 1.32 is now available!
status: added
date: 2025-03-24
category: containers
product: kubernetes
---

Scaleway managed Kubernetes products will support the latest minor Kubernetes version 1.32, Penelope, until May 2026.

In addition to patch versions `1.32.0`, `1.32.1`, `1.32.2`, releases `1.31.4`, `1.31.5`, `1.31.6`, and releases `1.30.4` to `1.30.10` are also available. Read our [version support policy](/kubernetes/reference-content/version-support-policy/) for more details on the 14-month support timeline.
