---
title: Increased Volume Capacity to 15TB
status: changed
date: 2026-03-09
category: storage
product: block-storage
---

It is now possible to provision individual Block Storage volumes with up to 15TB of capacity, up from the previous limit of 10TB.

This update is designed to better support data-intensive workloads with a need of increased vertical scaling. 


This increase applies to all new and existing 5K and 15K Block Storage volumes across all available regions. You can scale your existing volumes directly through the Scaleway Console, via the API, or using the CLI.

For more details on managing your storage, refer to our [Block Storage documentation](https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/block-storage/).
