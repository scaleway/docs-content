Autorenew is a feature that allows you, on a yearly basis, to automatically renew the validity period of a domain name registered with Scaleway Domains and DNS.

<Macro id="requirements" />

- A Scaleway account logged into the [console](https://console.scaleway.com/)
- [Owner](/identity-and-access-management/iam/concepts/#owner) status or [IAM permissions](/identity-and-access-management/iam/concepts/#permission) allowing you to perform actions in the intended Organization
- A domain name registered with [Scaleway Domains and DNS](https://www.scaleway.com/en/domains-and-dns/)

## How to activate autorenew

1. Click **Domains and DNS** in the **Network** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. The Domains and DNS dashboard displays with a list of your internal domains.
2. Click the domain name you want to configure.
3. Click **Activate** under **Autorenew** in the domain information section. A pop-up displays asking you to confirm.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-domain-autorenew.webp" alt="" />
4. Click **Activate autorenew** to confirm.

You have activated autorenew for your domain name.

## How to deactivate autorenew

1. Click **Domains and DNS** in the **Network** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. The Domains and DNS dashboard displays with a list of your internal domains.
2. Click the domain name you want to configure.
3. Click **Deactivate** under **Autorenew** in the domain information section.
4. Click **Deactivate autorenew** in the pop-up to confirm.

You have deactivated autorenew for your domain name.
