---
title: More granular billing
status: changed
date: 2026-04-16
category: ai-data
product: generative-apis
---

Generative APIs billing is performed in **slices of 1,000 tokens** (instead of the previous slices of 1,000,000 tokens) starting **April 20th**.
Prices **do not** change with this update.
After this change, for a similar token usage, any bill will remain **the same or may be slightly lower**.
