## What are PRO2 Instances?

Scaleway's latest generation PRO2 Instances provide a secure, scalable, and rock-solid foundation for your production computing projects.

PRO2 Instances are the second generation of General Purpose Instances, after GP1. They provide production-grade performance, enriched Instance options, and the best price/performance ratio on the market.

## What are the benefits to expect from PRO2 Instances?

PRO2 Instances provide multiple specifications to meet different needs and use cases. The configurations available range from 2 vCPU and 8 GB of RAM (PRO2-XXS) to 32 vCPU and 128 GB of RAM (PRO2-L).

PRO2 Instances leverage [Block Storage](/compute/instances/concepts/#block-volumes), and enhance your data security and resiliency with features such as snapshots and 3x replication of the Instances.

PRO2 Instances are fully compatible with our ecosystem: they work with Object Storage, Public Gateways, Private Networks, Databases, Managed Kubernetes, etc.

</Concept>
