We are delighted to add L40S Instances in `waw-2`, fulfilling the GPU portfolio with L4 and H100 Instances.

Key features include:
- Nvidia L40S 48 GB (Ada Lovelace architecture)
- 4th generation Tensor cores
- 4th generation RT cores (graphics capability)
- Transformer Engine
- Available in multi-GPU 1, 2, 4 or 8 GPUs
