  ## IPAM
  ### IPAM supported endpoints
    The following [IPAM](/ipam/) endpoints are supported and will be logged in Audit Trail:

    - BookIP (`POST /ipam/v1/regions/{region}/ips`)
    - ReleaseIP (`DELETE /ipam/v1/regions/{region}/ips/{ip_id}`)
    - ReleaseIPSet (`POST /ipam/v1/regions/{region}/ip-sets/release`)
    - UpdateIP (`PATCH /ipam/v1/regions/{region}/ips/{ip_id}`)
    - AttachIP (`POST /ipam/v1/regions/{region}/ips/{ip_id}/attach`)
    - DetachIP (`POST /ipam/v1/regions/{region}/ips/{ip_id}/detach`)
    - MoveIP (`POST /ipam/v1/regions/{region}/ips/{ip_id}/move`)i
</Accordion>
