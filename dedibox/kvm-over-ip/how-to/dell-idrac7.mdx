This page shows you how to use [KVM](/dedibox/kvm-over-ip/concepts/#kvm-over-ip) on a Dedibox with DELL iDRAC 7.

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Dedibox console](https://console.online.net)
  - You have [Java installed](https://www.java.com/en/download/help/download_options.html) on your local computer
  - Your Dedibox server has a [Dell iDRAC 7](https://www.dell.com/support/manuals/en-us/integrated-dell-remote-access-cntrllr-7-v1.50.50/idrac7ug1.50.50-v1/overview) KVM-over-IP device
</Message>

## Creating an iDRAC 7 session

1. Click **Server** > **Server List** in your Dedibox console. The list of your dedicated servers displays.
2. Click the name of the server you want to access. The server's status page displays.
3. Click **iDRAC** in the menu on the right. A pop-up displays.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway_idrac.webp" alt="" />
4. Accept the terms for iDRAC access by clicking **I Accept**.
5. Enter the authorized IPv4 address for the iDRAC connection. The IP address of your Internet connection is already pre-filled in the form. Then click **Create** to generate your credentials.
    <Message type="note">
      Currently, only IPv4 addresses are accepted.
    </Message>

The connection URL and your credentials display. Click on the link to access the iDRAC interface.

## Accessing the KVM-over-IP device 

1. Open the connection URL of your iDRAC in your web browser, then log in using the credentials displayed during access creation. 
2. Click **Attached Media** in the top menu. The virtual media page displays.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway_idrac7_dashboard.webp" alt="" />
3. Enter the image file path in the form and click **Connect**.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway_idrac7_remote_share.webp" alt="" />

    <Message type="tip">
    You can find the image file path for your OS on our [NAS](http://virtualmedia.online.net). <br />
    Do not hesitate to contact our support team if your favorite OS is missing from the NAS.
    </Message>
4. Click **Properties** (A), then **Launch** (B) to start the virtual console.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway_idrac7_console.webp" width="" />
5. Reboot the server, then press **F11** during the boot process to enter the boot manager.
6. Click **BIOS Boot Menu**.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway_idrac7_bios_boot.webp" width="" />
7. Click **iDRAC Virtual CD**.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway_idrac7_virtual_cd.webp" width="" />


Your server boots from the ISO file on the NAS and you can complete the installation of your operating system.
