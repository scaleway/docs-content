## How to create a folder and a secret

<Message type="note">
  If you have no resources yet in Secret Manager, you can create a folder during secret creation.
</Message>

1. Click **Secret Manager** in the **Security and Identity** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu.
2. Click **+ Create secret**.
3. Choose the region in which you want to store your folder and your secret.
    <Message type="important">
     - Your folder and secret will be duplicated in all the Availability Zones of the selected region.
     - Folders and secrets cannot be moved from one region to another after creation.
    </Message>
4. Select **Create a new path** and enter `/`, followed by a name for your [folder](/identity-and-access-management/secret-manager/concepts/#folder).
    <Message type="important">
      A [path](/identity-and-access-management/secret-manager/concepts/#path) is the directory structure to access your folders and secrets. Each path **must be prefixed** with a slash.
    </Message>
5. Enter a name for your secret and add tags (optional).
6. Add your secret manually or import it from a file.
    <Message type="note">
     The maximum file size for your secret is 64 KiB.
    </Message>
6. Click **Create secret**. The **Overview** tab of your secret displays. You can copy your secret's ID, add a description, tags or delete your secret.
7. Click **< Back to Secrets** to display your folder and its contents.

    <Message type="tip">
      - Once you have created resources in Secret Manager, you can create more folders by selecting your desired region in the **Region** drop-down, clicking the **Create folder** button and completing the folder creation procedure.
      - Read our [documentation](/identity-and-access-management/secret-manager/api-cli/migrate-secrets-in-folders/) to find out how to migrate secrets located in the `/(root)` path, to specific folders.
    </Message>

## How to create and manage a secret version

1. Click **Secret Manager** in the **Security and Identity** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. Your resources located in the `/(root)` path of the Paris region display.
2. Access the secret you want to add a version to. Your secret's **Overview** tab displays.
3. Click the **Versions** tab.
4. Click **+ Create version**. A pop-up displays.
5. Add your version manually or import it from a file.
6. Click <Icon name="toggle" /> to enable or disable the version.
7. Click **Create version**. Your secret versions display.
8. Click <Icon name="more" /> next to the version you want to manage.
9. Click **Disable** to disable one specific version of your secret.
10. Click **Copy version's value** to copy the value of your version.
