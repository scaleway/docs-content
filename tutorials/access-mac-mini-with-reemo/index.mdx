## Reeemo - Overview

[Reemo](https://reemo.io/) is a remote desktop solution to access your computers from anywhere on any device without any configuration. 
Once the Reemo service is installed on the remote device you can acccess it from any device running a Chrome browser. 

<Message type="requirement">

You have:
- You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com)
- You have [created a Mac mini M1](/compute/apple-silicon/how-to/create-mac-mini-m1/)
</Message>

## Creating your Reemo account.

<Message type="tip">

A Reemo Cloud account account is required to access your Mac mini M1 via the Reemo service. Reemo Cloud is **free** for personal use.

</Message>

1. Go to the [Reemo portal](https://portal.reemo.io/) and click **Get started** to create your Reemo account.

  <Lightbox="scaleway-reemo-portal.png" alt="" />

2. Enter your email address, a password and its confirmation in the form. Then tick the checkbox to agree Reemo's terms of service and click **Contine** to create your account. You will receive an email with a confirmation code.

3. Open your email account and check your security code. Then enter the code in the form and click **Validate** to confirm the account creation. 

  <Lightbox="scaleway-reemo-validate.png" alt="" />

  <Message type="tip">

  If you do not receive the validation code, check your spam folder. If you still cannot find the code, click **Send again** to receive it again.

  </Message>

4. Click **My private key** to display your private key which you need during the installation of the Reemo client on your Mac mini M1. 

  <Message type="important">

  Keep this code secret and don't share it.

  </Message>

## Installing the Reemo client

1. Log into your Mac mini M1. You can [connect](/compute/apple-silicon/how-to/connect-to-mac-mini-m1/) to your machine either using the VNC client or via SSH. 

2. If you are logged into the Mac mini M1 via VNC, open a terminal. If you are connected via SSH you can skip this step.

3. Run the following command to install the Reemo client on your machine:
  ```sh 
  curl -skL -o /tmp/install_reemo.x 'https://download.reemo.io/macos/setup.x' && sudo bash /tmp/install_reemo.x
  ```
  <Message type="note">

  You must be logged into the administrator account of your Mac mini M1 to run the command above.

  </Message>

4. Enter the **private key** of your Reemo account when prompted. Press Enter to validate.

The Reemo client is installed now on your Mac mini M1 and the machine appears on the Reemo dashboard.

## Connecting to your Mac mini M1 using Reemo

1. Open the [Reemo portal](https://portal.reemo.io/) in a [Chrome](https://www.google.com/chrome/) browser on a device of your chocice. 

  <Message type="tip">

  Reemo works on any device capable of running the Chrome browser. This could be your PC, tablet, phone or any other device running Chrome.

  </Message>

2. Click on your Mac mini M1 to open a session. The remote connection is established directly from your Chrome browser.

  <Lightbox src="scaleway-reemo-dashboard.png" alt="" />

3. Log in your macOS using your credentials.

You can now use your remote desktop.
