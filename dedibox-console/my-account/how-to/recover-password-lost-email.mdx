For security reasons it is recommended that you verify your contact details regularly and update your email address in case it changes. This will prevent you from being locked out from your account in case you have lost both, your Console password and access to your email account. 
In case this unlikely situation arrives, you can follow the following procedure to recover access to your Scaleway Dedibox account. 

<Message type="requirement">

  - You have an Scaleway Dedibox account

</Message>

In case you can no longer access your email account, you must send us a request to update your contact information with the following pieces of identification:

* Email address
* Postal address
* Two different documents proofing your identity (passport or local ID card for individual accounts + incorporation certificated for companies)
* A phone number to contact you
* Copy of your last Scaleway Dedibox invoice or the amount charged if you donâ€™t have a copy
* Card statement showing the Online by Scaleway bill debit

Once you have gathered all the required documents, the request has to be sent by email to: document@scaleway.com

We will update the e-mail address on receipt of the application, subject to validation.

Once your email address has been updated, you can use [this form](https://console.online.net/en/user/recovery) to receive the recovery codes for your account.
