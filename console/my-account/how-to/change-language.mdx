You can choose which language you would like to view the [Scaleway console](/console/my-account/concepts/#console) in. This language will also be used for any marketing and product communications Scaleway sends you.

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
</Message>


1. Log into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com).
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-console-menu.webp" alt="" />
2. Click your Organization name in the top right corner of the console to open the Organization dropdown menu (A).1. Log into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com).
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-console-menu.webp" alt="" />
2. Click your Organization name in the top right corner of the console to open the Organization dropdown menu (A).
4. Click the **Edit Profile** button. The **Edit Profile** screen displays:
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-edit-profile.webp" alt="" />
5. Choose your preferred language from the drop-down list, and click **Confirm Changes**.

    Your console will now be displayed in your preferred language, and you will also receive marketing and product communications in this language. You can repeat this procedure to change your preferred language at any time.
