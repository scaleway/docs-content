<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [registered a domain name](/network/domains-and-dns/how-to/register-internal-domain/) at Scaleway or another registrar
  - You have a Webhosting service
</Message>

1. Click **Webhosting** in the **Managed Services** section of the [console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. The **Webhosting** overview page displays.
2. Click <Icon name="seeMore" /> or the domain name of the Webhosting service you want to configure. The **Hosting information** page displays.
3. Scroll down to the **Terminate Webhosting** section. Click **Terminate**. A pop-up displays.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-webhosting-terminate.webp" alt="" />
4. Type **TERMINATE** and click **Terminate Webhosting** to confirm the termination of the Webhosting.
    <Message type="important">
        This action is irrevisible and it will permanently delete your Webhosting account and all your data (files and emails) will be lost.
    </Message>
