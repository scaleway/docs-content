If you forget or lose the password to your [Scaleway account](https://console.scaleway.com), there are several methods you can use to regain access.

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have a [Scaleway](https://www.scaleway.com/en/elements) account
</Message>

## Passwordless authentication using a Magic Link

The [Magic Link](/console/my-account/how-to/sign-in-with-magic-link/) allows you to access your account without a password, using multifactor authentication.

Click **Send a Magic Link** in the Login page of the Scaleway console.

<Lightbox src="scaleway-send-a-magic-link.webp" />

A secret link is sent to your email. You can access your account by clicking on the link.

## Password reset using email validation

If you forget or lose your password, you can request to reset it.

1. Go to the [lost password](https://console.scaleway.com/forgot-password/) page of the console:
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-forgotten-password-screen.webp" />
2. Enter your email address and click **Reset my password**. A password reset link is sent to your email address.
3. Click the link in the email address to reset your password.
4. Enter your new password and click **Reset password** to set the new password for your account. You are redirected to the Login page.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-password-reset.webp" />
5. Log in to the console by using your email address and the newly set password.

## Password recovery in case of lost email account

If you lose access to your email account, it will not be possible to reset the password directly from the Scaleway console.

In case you can no longer access your email account, you must send us a request to update your contact information with the following pieces of identification:

* Email address
* Postal address
* Two different documents proving your identity (passport or local ID card for individual accounts + incorporation certificated for companies)
* A phone number to contact you
* Copy of your last Scaleway invoice or the amount charged if you do not have a copy
* A card or bank statement showing your Scaleway invoice debit

Once you have gathered all the required documents, the request has to be sent by email to: [document@scaleway.com](mailto:document@scaleway.com)

We will update the email address on receipt of the application, subject to validation.

Once your email address has been updated, you can use the [lost password form](https://console.scaleway.com/forgot-password) to receive the recovery codes for your account.

## Password security guidelines

Besides the usual guidelines you should follow to create a complex password (must contain UPPERCASE and lowercase letters, symbols (`@`, `$`, `&`, â€¦) and numbers), some basic rules exist to keep your password - and your account - safe:

  - Never give your password to anyone. Especially not via email, IRC, Slack, WhatsApp, or any messaging system. Scaleway employees will never ask you for your password.
  - Use [multifactor authentication](https://console.scaleway.com/account/profile) to add an extra layer of security.
  - Do not write down your password.
  - Always log out from your account at the end of each session, or if you are away from your computer.
