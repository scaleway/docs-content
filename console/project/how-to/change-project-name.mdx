<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [created a Project](/console/project/how-to/create-a-project) other than the [default Project](/console/project/concepts/#default-project)
</Message>

<Message type="important">
  The name of the default Project cannot be changed.
</Message>

1. Click the **Projects** tab from the [Organization Dashboard](https://console.scaleway.com/organization). A list of your Projects displays.
2. Click the name of the Project you want to edit.
3. Click the **Settings** tab.
4. Click **Change Project name**. A pop-up appears.
5. Enter the new name of your Project.
6. Click **Change Project name**.
