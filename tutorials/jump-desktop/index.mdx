## Jump Desktop - Overview

[Jump Desktop](https://jumpdesktop.com) is a secure and reliable remote desktop infrastructure that lets you connect to any computer, anywhere in the world without VPNs or gateways. Jump Desktop is designed to work perfectly in iOS, Mac, Windows and Android devices.

Scaleway's Apple silicon M1 as-a-Service offer is highly compatible with Jump Desktop. Scaleway's Mac mini M1 is built using the latest generation of Apple Mac mini hardware. These dedicated Mac mini M1 are designed for developing, building, testing, and signing applications for Apple devices, including iPhones, iPads, Mac computers and much more. Jump Desktop can be installed easily on Scaleway's Mac mini M1 which allows you to connect to your M1 remotely while keeping its performance and efficiency.

In this tutorial, you will learn how to launch your Mac mini M1 development environment on Jump Desktop.

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />
 
<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [created a Mac mini M1](/bare-metal/apple-silicon/how-to/create-mac-mini-m1)
  - You have installed a [VNC viewer](https://www.tightvnc.com/) on your local computer. 
</Message>

## Downloading Jump Desktop on your local computer 

1. Type **https://jumpdesktop.com** in your browser's search bar. 
2. Scroll down to the middle of the page.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-middle-of-the-page.webp" alt="os to choose jump dekstop" />
3. Download the Jump Desktop version that is compatible with your operating system. 

## Downloading Jump Desktop Connect on your Mac mini M1

From the [Mac mini M1 list in your Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com/asaas/servers): 

1. Click the Mac mini M1 you want to access remotely.
2. Click the **VNC** button. 
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-vnc-access.webp" alt="vnc button to access mac mini" />
3. Choose a screen sharing application to open your Mac mini M1. 
4. Open an internet browser on your Mac mini M1 and type **[https://jumpdesktop.com/connect](https://jumpdesktop.com/connect)** in your browser's search bar.
5. Choose your operating system. 
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-jump-desktop-connect.webp" alt="download jump desktop connect" />
6. Click **Download Jump Desktop Connect**. 
7. Go to **Overview** tab of your Mac mini M1  and copy the VNC password. 
8. Enter the VNC password to continue the installation.
    <Message type="note">
      If you are on Mac, you will be asked to give access permissions to the Jump Desktop appplication. 
    </Message>


## Connecting your local computer with your Mac mini M1

**On your Mac mini M1:**

1. Launch the Jump Desktop Connect application. 
2. Click **Add a remote access user**. 
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-add-remote-access-user.webp" alt="add remote access user " />
3. Click the link in the **Add a user** window to sign in via your web browser.

**On your local computer:**

1. Launch the Jump Desktop application. 
2. Click **File** on the top menu. 
3. Click **Sign In** on the drop down menu.
4. Sign in with the account used to setup Jump Desktop Connect on the Mac mini M1. 
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-jump-desktop-remote.webp" alt="jump dekstop remote" />

    <Message type="note">
    Your remote computer is now displayed in Jump Desktop application and ready to be accessed.

    </Message>


## Accessing your Mac mini M1 via Jump Desktop

**On your local computer:**

1. Launch the Jump Desktop application. 
2. Double click your Mac mini M1 remote computer.

    Your Mac mini M1 will be displayed.
