You now have access to Transactional Email (TEM) dashboard in your Scaleway's product dashboards folder in Grafana.
Metrics available :
- Current domain reputation
- Reputation score evolution over time
- Total email sent
- Total email drop
- General activity overview over time
- Error activity over time
- Top recipients destinations
- Top bounce recipient
- Top fail recipient
