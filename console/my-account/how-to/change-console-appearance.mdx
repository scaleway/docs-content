You can choose the the color scheme of the [Scaleway console](/console/my-account/concepts/#console) according to your preferences. We offer a light, dark or system based color scheme. 

 <Message type="requirement">

  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com) 

</Message>

1. Click on your name in the top right corner of the Scaleway console. A pop-up menu opens. 

2. Click **My Profile** to view your Scaleway user profile. 

    <Lightbox src="scaleway-console-my-profile.webp" alt="" /> 

3. Scroll to the **Appearance** section and select the color scheme for your Scaleway console. You can choose from three options:
    * A light color scheme 
    * A dark color scheme
    * A color scheme based on the your system preferences

    <Lightbox src="scaleway-console-appearance.webp" alt="" />

    <Message type="note">

    Your selection is saved automatically after you have chosen a color scheme. 

    </Message>
