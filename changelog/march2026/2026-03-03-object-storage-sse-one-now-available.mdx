---
title: SSE-ONE encryption is now available for Object Storage
status: added
date: 2026-03-03
category: storage
product: object-storage
---

SSE-ONE provides automatic server-side encryption of your objects using keys provided by Scaleway, delivering a straightforward and fully managed encryption experience with zero operational overhead. Simply enable SSE-ONE at bucket creation, or from the settings of an existing bucket, to protect your data at rest. All objects uploaded after activating SSE-ONE will be encrypted transparently.

Refer to our [dedicated documentation](/object-storage/how-to/enable-sse-one) for more information.
