The network information regarding your server can be found on your servers status page in the management console.

1. Log into your [management console](https://console.online.net/).
2. Click **Server** > **Server List** > **Select your server** > **Manage**.

## IP ranges used by Online.net

Currently we use the following IP ranges: `62.210.0.0/16` & `195.154.0.0/16` & `212.83.128.0/18`

IPs in the ranges `88.190.0.0/16` & `88.191.0.0/16` are **no longer in use**.

## Monitoring of your servers

Our monitoring servers are located in the IP subnet `62.210.16.0/24`.

## DNS cache servers

- IPv4: `62.210.16.6`
- IPv6: `2001:bc8:401::3`

**nscache-2.online.net**

- IPv4: `62.210.16.7`
