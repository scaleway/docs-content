If you have chosen to install Windows Server 2019 on your Dedibox, and wish to change the language, this document shows you how.

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Dedibox Console](https://console.online.net)
  - You have a [Dedibox server](/dedibox/dedicated-servers/how-to/order-a-server)
  - You have [installed](/dedibox/dedicated-servers/how-to/install-a-server) Windows Server 2019 on your server.
</Message>

1. Log onto your Windows Server's graphical interface (via Remote Desktop or similar).
2. Click the Start menu and then the gear icon to open the Settings Interface.
3. Click **Time & Language** (**Heure et langue** in French)
4. Click **Language** in the side panel on the left (**Langue** in French)
5. Click **Add a language** on the Language screen (**Ajouter une langue** in French). The **Choose a language to install** (**Choisir une langue à installer**) screen displays.
6. Select a language from the list, and click **Next** (**Suivant**). The **Install language features** (**Installer des fonctionnalités linguistiques**) screen displays.
7. Check the box next to the features you want, and click **Install** (**Installer**). The system connects to the Internet to download and install the language pack. 

    When the process is finished you are alerted. You will see the language under **Windows display language**. It may not take effect as the display language until after you reboot your server.
