This page shows how to delete your [Mac mini M1](/bare-metal/compute/apple-silicon/concepts/#mac-mini-m1).

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="important">
  Deleting your Mac mini M1 is an irreversible action. Any data stored on it will be lost.
</Message>

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [created a Mac mini M1](/bare-metal/compute/apple-silicon/how-to/create-mac-mini-m1/)
</Message>

1. Click **Apple silicon** in the **Compute** section of the side menu. A list of your Mac mini M1s displays.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-m1-list.webp" alt="" />
2. Click <Icon name="more" /> next to the Mac mini M1 you want to delete, and select **Delete** from the drop-down menu. A pop-up asks you to confirm the action.
3. Type **DELETE** and then click **Delete Mac mini M1**

You are returned to the list of your Mac mini M1s, where the machine you deleted no longer appears.
