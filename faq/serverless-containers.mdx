## How am I billed for Serverless Containers?

### Principles


Serverless Containers are billed on a pay as you go basis strictly on resources (Memory and CPU) consumptions. 

* **Memory consumption:** It is obtained by multiplying the memory tiers chosen by the container runtime
* **vCPU consumption:** it is obtained by multiplying the vCPU tiers chosen by the container runtime
