---
title: Cockpit data exporter is now in General Availability
status: changed
date: 2026-05-13
category: monitoring
product: cockpit
---

You can now export your Scaleway metrics and logs natively and in real-time to different destinations, including Datadog and any OpenTelemetry-compatible endpoint.

Data exports can now be managed from the Cockpit menu in the console for easier access. Check the [data exporter documentation](/cockpit/how-to/manage-data-exports/) if you need any help.
