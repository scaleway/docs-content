1. Click **Webhosting** in the **Managed Services** section of the [console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. The **Webhosting** overview page displays.
2. Click <icon name="seeMore" /> or the domain name of the webhosting you want to configure. The **Hosting information** page displays.
3. Scroll down to the **Terminate Webhosting** section. Click **Terminate**. A pop-up displays.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-webhosting-terminate.webp" alt="" />
4. Type **TERMINATE** and click **Terminate Webhosting** to confirm the termination of your Webhosting.
    <Message type="important">
        This action is irrevisible and it will permanently delete your Webhosting account and all your data (files and emails) will be lost.
    </Message>
