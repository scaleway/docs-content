---
title: Serverless Functions namespaces now have VPC enabled by default
status: changed
date: 2025-08-22
category: serverless
product: functions
---

Previously, Serverless Functions namespaces required the `VPC enabled` feature to work with Private Networks. Now, Private Networks are available for all Serverless Functions namespaces.

[How to use Private Networks with Serverless Functions](https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/serverless-functions/how-to/use-private-networks/)
