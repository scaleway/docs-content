You can now send XML as attachments in your transactional emails. The supported MIME are the following:
- `application/xml`
- `text/xml`

Find out about all the [attachment types available](https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/managed-services/transactional-email/reference-content/tem-capabilities-and-limits/).
