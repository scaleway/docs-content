Scaleway provides 24/7 basic technical support by ticket for free. You can contact the technical assistance directly from your console. You can [upgrade your support level](/console/my-account/how-to/configure-support-plans/) to profit from phone support and a dedicated account manager.

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
</Message>

If you have any inquiries and wish to speak to our Support team, you may open a Support ticket by clicking on **Support** in the lower left menu.

<Lightbox src="scaleway-support-in-the-menu.webp" alt="" />

Alternatively, you can click <Icon name="assistance" /> in the top menu. The [support center](https://console.scaleway.com/support/tickets) displays.

<Lightbox src="scaleway-support-center.webp" alt="" />

Click **+ Open a new ticket** to do so.


<Message type="important">
  If you have lost access to the Scaleway console and want to create a ticket, you must first [follow this procedure](/console/my-account/how-to/use-2fa/#how-to-regain-access-to-your-account) to regain access to your account. 
</Message>
