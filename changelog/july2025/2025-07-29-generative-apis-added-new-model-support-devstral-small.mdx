[Devstral Small 2505](https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/generative-apis/reference-content/supported-models/#chat-models) is now available in preview for Generative APIs.

Devstral Small is a fine-tuned version of Mistral Small 3.1, optimized to perform agentic coding tasks. It can be plugged [into your favorite IDE](/generative-apis/reference-content/integrating-generative-apis-with-popular-tools/).
