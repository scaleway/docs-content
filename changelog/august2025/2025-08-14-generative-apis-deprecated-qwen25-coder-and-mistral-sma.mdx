[Qwen2.5 Coder](/generative-apis/reference-content/supported-models/) and [Mistral Small 3.1](/generative-apis/reference-content/supported-models/) are now deprecated in favor of [Qwen3 Coder](/generative-apis/reference-content/supported-models/) and [Mistral Small 3.2](/generative-apis/reference-content/supported-models/).

Following our [model lifecycle policy](/generative-apis/reference-content/model-lifecycle/), both models will remain accessible up to November 14th, 2025. After this date, requests will be routed automatically to their most recent version.
