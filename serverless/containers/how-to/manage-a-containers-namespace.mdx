This page shows you how to manage your Serverless Containers namespace. [Namespaces](/network/domains-and-dns/concepts/#dns-namespace) allow you to group your containers and share environment variables between them.

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [created a containers namespace](/serverless/containers/how-to/create-a-containers-namespace/)
</Message>

1. Click **Containers** in the **Serverless** section of the side menu. The containers page displays.
2. Click the containers namespace you want to manage. 
3. Click the **Namespace settings** tab. Here you can view information about the region, registry endpoint and description of your namespace.
4. Add any additional environment variables you wish to add, by clicking **+Add**, entering the key/value pair, and clicking **Save**.
5. Add **secrets** for your container. Secrets are environment variables which are injected into your function, but the values are not retained or displayed by Scaleway after initial validation.
