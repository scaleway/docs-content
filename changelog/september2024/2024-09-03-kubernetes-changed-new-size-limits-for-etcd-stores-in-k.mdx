To maintain optimal performance of your Scaleway managed Kubernetes clusters, etcd stores in control planes are now subject to size limits: 55MB for mutualized control planes and 200MB for dedicated control planes.

For more details, please refer to our [dedicated documentation](https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/containers/kubernetes/reference-content/kubernetes-control-plane-offers/).
