An abuse ticket is a complaint about the practice of hosting copyrighted content or illegal behavior using Scaleway services.

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Dedibox Console](https://console.online.net) 
</Message>

1. Click your user name next to _logged in as:_ in the top right corner of the console. Then click **[Abuses](https://console-sudo.infra.online.net/en/account/abuses/list)** in the pop-up menu.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-dedibox-console-abuses-menu.webp" width="300" alt="" />

  A list of your abuse tickets displays.
2. Click **Details** to see the details of the complaint:
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-dedibox-console-abuses-list.webp" width="800" alt="" />
3. Take note of the details and complete the steps necessary to resolve the problem. You can find information on  how to resolve [outgoing DDoS](/compute/instances/reference-content/preventing-outgoing-ddos/) attacks from your server in our documentation.
4. Describe the measures you have taken and provide an answer to the author of the ticket once the issue is solved. Click **Resolve** to mark the ticket as closed.
    <Message type="note">
      More information about Scaleway's abuse policy can be found [here](https://www.scaleway.com/en/abuse-notice/).
    </Message>
