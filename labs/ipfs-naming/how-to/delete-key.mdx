<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
    - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/)
    - You have [created a key](/labs/ipfs-naming/how-to/create-key)
</Message>

1. Click **IPFS Naming** in the **Labs** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com) side menu. The IPFS Naming section displays a list of your records.
2. Click the resource name to access the record information page.
3. Click **Delete** under the **Delete** section of the page. A pop-up asks you to confirm the action.
4. Type `DELETE` to confirm.
    <Message type="important">
        This action is irreversible and will permanently delete the key and all its associated names.
    </Message>
5. Click **Delete name**. You are returned to the list of your names, where the name you deleted no longer appears.
