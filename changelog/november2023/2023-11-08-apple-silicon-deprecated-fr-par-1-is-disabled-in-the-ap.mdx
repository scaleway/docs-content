For Apple Silicon M1 servers, requests to `/apple-silicon/v1alpha1/zones/fr-par-1/*` will no longer receive responses. All requests should now go to the PAR-3 AZ, using `/apple-silicon/v1alpha1/zones/fr-par-3/*`.
