status: changed

The monthly price for the Standard One Zone class has been reduced from €0,0000165/GB/hour (approx. €0.012GB/month) to €0,0000103/GB/hour (approx. €0.0075/GB/month). 

The Standard One Zone storage class comes with a 99% availability SLA, making it ideal for cost-effective storage in production and development environments, media distribution, monitoring, and backup.
