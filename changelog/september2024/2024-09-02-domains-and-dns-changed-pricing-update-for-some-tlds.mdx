Starting Monday 2nd September, the following TLDs will have a new pricing for classic actions (buy, renew, transfer): .beauty, .college, .hair, .makeup, .monster, .quest, .rent, and .skin.

From Monday 9th September, .com and .net TLDs will also be impacted.

You can refer to this page to consult our new pricing : https://www.scaleway.com/en/domains-name/
