status: deprecated
date: 2026-07-02

Starting 1st July, 2026, Devstral 2, Voxtral Small, Gemma 3, Pixtral and Qwen 3 Coder on Generative APIs Serverless will be Deprecated and become End of Life in accordance with our [Model Lifecycle Policy](https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/generative-apis/reference-content/model-lifecycle/). After their End of Life date, each model request (except Voxtral Small) will be routed to a [model with similar pricing and capabilities](https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/generative-apis/reference-content/supported-models/#end-of-life-eol-models).
