## How to contact the support team by tickets? 

1. Log into your [Dedibox console](https://console.online.net) account. 
2. Click **Support**. 

     <Lightbox src="scaleway_create_ticket.png" alt="" />

3. Click **Technical assistance**. A form displays. 
4. Fill out the form. 
5. Once you have written your message, send the ticket. 
6. You will be able to visualize your conversation with the technical assistance team and see the history of your tickets. 


<Message type="note">

If you benefit from phone support, you can find the number to call as well as your identifier in your [Dedibox console](https://console.online.net). 

</Message>

<Message type="tip">

Take a look at the [Scaleway Status](https://status.scaleway.com/) page to check the status of our services and to find maintenance information. 

</Message>


## How to contact support via Slack? 

You can communicate with our clients and our team members through [Slack](https://slack.com). 

1. Create a [Slack account](https://slack.scaleway.com/) to access our chatrooms. You will receive an invite at the address you entered when you registered. 
2. Once you have validated your account, join our channels: 

    ```
    #community - English channel

    ```

    ```
    #community-fr - French channel 

    ```

3. Type in **/join #name_of_the_channel** to join a channel. 

## How to download the Slack client? 

1. Download a [client for Slack](https://slack.com/intl/en-us/downloads/) available for **Windows**, **Linux** and **MacOS**. 
2. A mobile client is also available for **Android**, **iOS**, and **Windows Phone** depending on the corresponding application stores.
