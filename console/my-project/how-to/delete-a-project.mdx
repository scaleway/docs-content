A project can be deleted only once it has been emptied of [resources](/console/my-project/concepts#resources). Therefore, **to delete your project, you must first remove all of its resources**.

<Message type="requirement">

- You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com)
- You have [created a project](/console/my-project/how-to/create-a-project) other than the [default project](/console/my-project/concepts#default-project)
- The project you want to delete has been emptied of resources.

</Message>

1. Click **Project Dashboard** in the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com) side menu. 

2. Click the **Settings** tab.

3. Scroll down to find the **Delete Project** section.
  <Lightbox src="scaleway-delete-project.png" alt="" />

4. Click **Delete Project**. A pop-up appears requesting you to confirm if you are sure you want to delete the selected project. 
  <Lightbox src="scaleway-delete-pop-up.png" alt="" />

5. Click **Delete this project** to confirm. 

You are redirected to the default project Dashboard.
