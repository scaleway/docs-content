The Partner Space is a dedicated section of the Scaleway console allowing partners to manage Scaleway Organizations for their clients.

## Console overview
Discover the Partner Space interface in the Scaleway console.
<GuideFlow src="https://app.guideflow.com/embed/gky99ezs4p"/>

<Requirements />

- A Scaleway account logged in to the [console](https://console.scaleway.com)
- Scaleway Partner status

## How to add a client

1. Click **Go to Partner Space** from your [Organization Dashboard](https://console.scaleway.com/organization).
2. Click **Add client**.
3. Complete the following steps:
    - Enter your client’s **company name**.
    - Enter your client’s **SIREN** number.
    - Enter the **client external identifier**. This refers to the customer ID in your internal tools.
    - Enter the **first name**, **last name**, **email address**, and **phone number** of a contact of your choice within the client company. Note that this person will receive an email inviting them to join the Organization that will be created.
4. Click **Add client** to confirm. The list of your clients displays.

### How to edit client information

1. Click <Icon name="more" /> to the right of the client’s creation date.
2. Edit the **client external identifier** and/or the **email address**.

    <Message type="note">
     You must edit the Client ID before the 25th of the current month to apply it to your next invoice.
    </Message>
3. Click **Apply changes** to confirm.

<Message type="important">
     **Getting access to support tickets**
     Note that it is not possible to access your customer’s support tickets, either to create or access them, from the Partner console. To create or consult a support ticket on behalf of one of your customers, you must log in to their Organization and visit their [Help Center](https://console.scaleway.com/support/tickets).
</Message>
