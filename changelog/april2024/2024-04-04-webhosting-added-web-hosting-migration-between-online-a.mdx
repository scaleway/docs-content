Good news for all our faithful clients. You can now migrate your Web Hosting from Online to Scaleway Elements. 
The migration process start in the old Online Console. You'll be invited to create a Scaleway account if you haven't one yet, and then migrate your hosting. 

The migration tool do every thing for you automatically if you also have domain at Scaleway/Online, you only have to choose a new offer !
