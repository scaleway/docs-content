You can now enable preconfigured alerts with Audit Trail events to get notified of abnormal behavior. We added a first set of alert rules and will add more in the future. Read [our documentation](/audit-trail/how-to/activate-adt-preconfigured-alerts/) to find out how to manage your alerts.
