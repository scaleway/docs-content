The existing free tier is evolving to better support our customers' intensive use cases. The brand new free trial offers you 750 GB of free Multi-AZ Standard class for 90 days. You can activate your Object Storage free trial directly from the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/object-storage/buckets/). To be eligible for this offer, you must have never used the Standard Multi-AZ class before December 1st, 2023. Be ready for a 90-day deep dive into Object Storage features, with special insights and onboarding tips!
