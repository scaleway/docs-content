A namespace's privacy policy specifies whether everyone is everyone is granted access to pull its images or not. Everyone will be able to pull container images from a public namespace.

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com) 
  - You have created a [Container Registry namespace](/compute/container-registry/how-to/create-namespace)
  - You have [pushed an image](/compute/container-registry/how-to/push-images) to your namespace 
</Message>

1. Click **Container Registry** in the **Tools** section of the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com) side menu. The Container Registry namespaces dashboard displays.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-registry_namespace_list.webp" />
2. Click the namespace's name to list its images. 
3. Click the **Namespace Settings** tab, then configure the policy by selecting either **Public** or **Private**.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-registry_namespace_privacy_policies.webp" />

<Message type="note">
  Each container image may additionally have its [own privacy policies](/compute/container-registry/how-to/manage-image-privacy-settings).
</Message>
