You can choose the language in which you would like to view the [Scaleway console](/account/concepts/#console). This language will also be used for any marketing and product communications Scaleway sends you.

<Requirements />

- A [Scaleway account](https://console.scaleway.com)

1. Access the [Settings space](https://console.scaleway.com/settings/) of your console.
2. Click [Language](https://console.scaleway.com/settings/account/profile#profile-language) under the **Account** section of the side menu.
3. Select your preferred language from the drop-down list.

The console will now be displayed in your chosen language. You can change your preferred language at any time by repeating this procedure.
