<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Elements Console](https://console.scaleway.com/)
  - You have [created an Instance](/compute/instances/how-to/create-an-instance/)
  - You have [created at least one snapshot](/compute/instances/how-to/create-a-snapshot/)
</Message>


1. Log into your [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com/) account.
2. Click **Instances** in the side menu. The **Instances** page displays.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway_instances_overview.webp" alt="" />
3. Click the **Images** dashboard. A list of your images displays.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway_list_images.webp" alt="" />
4. Hover over the <Icon name="plus"/> button to **Create image**. The overview page displays.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway_create_an_image.webp" alt="" />
5. Click the Availability Zone in which you want your image to be created.
6. Enter a name for your image.
    <Message type="note">
      Your image's name can only contain alphanumeric characters and dashes.
    </Message>
7. Select the snapshot from which you want to create your image in the drop-down box.
8. Click **Create image** to create your image. 
    <Lightbox src="scaleway_last_button.webp" alt="" />


<Message type="tip">
  You can also create an image from the **Snapshots** dashboard by clicking <Icon name="more"/> next to the snapshot from which you want to create an image. Click **Image from snapshot**. A new tab opens : enter a name for your image. Then click **Create image from snapshot**.

  <Lightbox src="scaleway_createimg_snapshot.webp" alt="" />
</Message>
