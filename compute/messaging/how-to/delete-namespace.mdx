This page shows how to delete a [Messaging namespace](/compute/messaging/concepts#namespace).

<Message type="requirement">

  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [created a Messaging namespace](/compute/messaging/how-to/create-namespace/)

</Message>

## How to delete a namespace

1. Click **Messaging** in the **Serverless** section of the side menu. The list of your Messaging namespaces displays.

2. Click the <Icon name="more" /> button next to the namespace you want to update. A menu displays.

  <Lightbox src="scaleway-namespace-menu.webp" alt="" />

4. Select **Delete**. A popup displays.

  <Lightbox src="scaleway-delete-namespace.webp" alt="" />

  <Message type="important">

    Deleting a namespace is irreversable. Any URLs, credentials and queued messages belonging to this namespace will also be deleted.

  </Message>

5. Type **DELETE** to confirm, and click **Delete namespace**.

  Your namespace is deleted. You are returned to your Messaging overview page.
