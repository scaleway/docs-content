This page shows you how to display your email activity and filter your emails using the [Scaleway console.](https://console.scaleway.com/)

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/).
  - You have [configured your domain(s) with Transactional Email](/managed-services/transactional-email/how-to/configure-domain-with-transactional-email).
  - You have [added SPF and DKIM records](/managed-services/transactional-email/how-to/add-spf-dkim-records-to-your-domain) to your domain(s).
  - You have [added and MX record](/managed-services/transactional-email/how-to/add-mx-record-to-your-domain) to your domain(s).
</Message>

## How to display your email activity

1. Click **Transactional Email** in the **Managed Services** section of the [console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. Your domains display.
2. Click the domain for which you want to track the email activity. You are redirected to the **Email activity** tab.
3. Click <Icon name="seeMore" /> next to your domain to get more detail about your email activity. Your domain’s email activity page displays.

<Message type="note">
 Your domain’s email activity page displays information such as:
 - the status of your email
 - the sender of the email
 - the number of attempts made to send your email
 - your domain’s recent events
</Message>

## How to filter your email activity

1. Click **Transactional Email** in the **Managed Services** section of the [console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. Your domains display.
2. Click the domain for which you want to filter the email activity. You are redirected to the **Email activity** tab.
3. Select the filters you want to apply in the **Filter by** search bar.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway_filter_email_activity.webp" alt="" />
4. Click **Apply filters** once you have selected which filters you want to apply. Your email activity displays according to the filters you have applied.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway_filtered_emails.webp" alt="" />
5. Optionally, click **Reset filters** when you are done filtering your email activity.
   <Lightbox src="scaleway_reset_filters.webp" alt="" />
