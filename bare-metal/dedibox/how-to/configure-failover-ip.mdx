<Macro id="requirements" />

- A Scaleway account logged into the [console](https://console.scaleway.com)
- [Owner](/iam/concepts/#owner) status or [IAM permissions](/iam/concepts/#permission) allowing you to perform actions in the intended Organization
- [Linked](/dedibox/how-to/link-dedibox-account/) your Dedibox and Scaleway accounts
- Ordered a [Dedibox server](/dedibox/how-to/order-dedibox/) and a [failover IP](/dedibox/how-to/order-failover-ip/)

The following pages provide information on how to configure a [failover IP](/ip-failover/concepts/#failover-ips) on Dedibox servers running various operating systems:

* [CentOS](/ip-failover/how-to/configure-centos/)
* [Debian and Ubuntu Linux](/ip-failover/how-to/configure-debian-ubuntu/)
* [FreeBSD](/ip-failover/how-to/configure-freebsd/)
* [Windows Server](/ip-failover/how-to/configure-windows/)

Learn more about failover IPs with our [dedicated documentation](/ip-failover/quickstart/).
