  ## Public Gateways

  ### Public Gateways supported endpoints

    The following [Public Gateways](/public-gateways/) endpoints are supported and will be logged in Audit Trail:

    - CreateGateway (`POST /vpc-gw/v1/zones/{zone}/gateways`)
    - UpdateGateway (`PATCH /vpc-gw/v1/zones/{zone}/gateways/{gateway_id}`)
    - DeleteGateway (`DELETE /vpc-gw/v1/zones/{zone}/gateways/{gateway_id}`)
    - UpgradeGateway (`POST /vpc-gw/v1/zones/{zone}/gateways/{gateway_id}/upgrade`)
    - CreateGatewayNetwork (`POST /vpc-gw/v1/zones/{zone}/gateway-networks`)
    - UpdateGatewayNetwork (`PATCH /vpc-gw/v1/zones/{zone}/gateway-networks/{gateway_network_id}`)
    - DeleteGatewayNetwork (`DELETE /vpc-gw/v1/zones/{zone}/gateway-networks/{gateway_network_id}`)
    - CreatePatRule (`POST /vpc-gw/v1/zones/{zone}/pat-rules`)
    - UpdatePatRule (`PATCH /vpc-gw/v1/zones/{zone}/pat-rules/{pat_rule_id}`)
    - SetPatRules (`PUT /vpc-gw/v1/zones/{zone}/pat-rules`)
    - DeletePatRule (`DELETE /vpc-gw/v1/zones/{zone}/pat-rules/{pat_rule_id}`)
    - CreateIP (`POST /vpc-gw/v1/zones/{zone}/ips`)
    - UpdateIP (`PATCH /vpc-gw/v1/zones/{zone}/ips/{ip_id}`)
    - DeleteIP (`DELETE /vpc-gw/v1/zones/{zone}/ips/{ip_id}`)
    - RefreshSSHKeys (`POST /vpc-gw/v1/zones/{zone}/gateways/{gateway_id}/refresh-ssh-keys`)
    - AddBastionAllowedIPs (`POST /vpc-gw/v2/zones/{zone}/gateways/{gateway_id}/bastion-allowed-ips`)
    - SetBastionAllowedIPs (`PUT /vpc-gw/v2/zones/{zone}/gateways/{gateway_id}/bastion-allowed-ips`)
    - DeleteBastionAllowedIPs (`DELETE /vpc-gw/v2/zones/{zone}/gateways/{gateway_id}/bastion-allowed-ips/{ip_range}`)
</Accordion>
