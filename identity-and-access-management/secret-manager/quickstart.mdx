## How to create a folder

1. Click **Secret Manager** in the **Security and Identity** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu.
2. Click **Create folder**.
3. Enter a name for your folder and click **Create folder**. Your newly created folder displays.

## How to create a subfolder

1. [Create a folder](#how-to-create-a-folder).
2. Click the folder in which you want to create a subfolder.
3. Click **Create folder**.
4. Enter a name for your subfolder.
5. Click **Create folder**. Your subfolder displays inside your folder.

## How to create a secret

1. Click **Secret Manager** in the **Security and Identity** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. The **Secret Manager** overview page displays.
2. Click **Create secret**.
3. Configure your secret:
    * Select the region in which you want to create your secret. Your secret will be duplicated in all the Availability Zones of the selected region.
    * Choose the [path](/identity-and-access-management/secret-manager/concepts/#path/) in which you want to store your secret, or create a new path.
    * Enter a name for your secret.
    * Optionally, add tags.
    * Add your secret manually or click <Icon name="plus" /> to import it from a file.
    <Message type="note">
     The maximum file size for your secret is 64 KiB.
    </Message>
4. Click **Create secret**.

## How to manage a secret

1. Click **Secret Manager** in the **Security and Identity** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. Your newly created secrets display.
2. Click <Icon name="more" /> next to the secret you want to manage.
3. Click **More info**. Your secret's overview page displays.
4. Click **Copy** to copy your secret's ID.
5. Click the **Tags** bar and add more tags to organize your secret.
6. Copy the code examples for the applications from which you want to get your secret.

## How to create a version of a secret

1. Click **Secret Manager** in the **Security and Identity** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. Your secrets display.
2. Click the secret you want to add a version to. Your secret's overview page displays.
3. Click the **Versions** tab.
4. Click <Icon name="plus" /> and **Create version**. A pop-up displays.
5. Add your version manually or click <Icon name="plus" /> to import it from a file.
6. Click <Icon name="toggle" /> to enable or disable the version.
7. Click ** version**.

## How to manage a version of a secret

1. Click **Secret Manager** in the **Security and Identity** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. Your secrets display.
2. Click the secret you want to add a version to. Your secret's overview page displays.
3. Click the **Versions** tab. The versions of your secret display.
4. Click <Icon name="more" /> next to the version you want to manage.
5. Click **Disable** to disable one specific version of your secret.
6. Click **Copy version's value** to copy the value of your version.

## How to delete your secret

1. Click **Secret Manager** in the **Security and Identity** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. Your secrets display.
2. Click <Icon name="more" /> next the secret you want to delete.
3. Click **Delete**. A pop-up displays.
4. Type **DELETE** and click **Delete secret**.

<Message type="important">
  Deleting a secret is permanent. All of the versions you have created for your secret will be deleted.
</Message>

## How to delete a version of a secret

1. Click **Secret Manager** in the **Security and Identity** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. Your secrets display.
2. Click the secret for which you want to delete the version. Your secret's overview page displays.
3. Click the **Versions** tab.
4. Click <Icon name="more" /> next to the version you want to delete.
5. Click **Delete**. A pop-up displays.
6. Type **DELETE** and click **Delete version**.

<Message type="important">
  Deleting a version is permanent. You will not be able to use the version again.
</Message>

## How to delete a folder

1. Click **Secret Manager** in the **Security and Identity** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. Your secrets and folders display.
2. Click the <Icon name="delete" /> icon next to the folder you want to delete.
3. Type **DELETE** to confirm and click **Delete folder**.

<Message type="important">
  This will delete all subfolders and secrets you might have created in the folder.
</Message>
