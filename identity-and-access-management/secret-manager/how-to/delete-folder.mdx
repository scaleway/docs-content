This page shows you how to delete a [folder](/identity-and-access-management/secret-manager/concepts/#folder) using the Scaleway console.

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [generated an API key](/identity-and-access-management/iam/how-to/create-api-keys/) and enabled the `SecretManagerFullAccess` [permission set](/identity-and-access-management/iam/reference-content/permission-sets/)
  - You have [created a folder](/identity-and-access-management/secret-manager/how-to/create-folder/)
</Message>


1. Click **Secret Manager** in the **Security and Identity** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu.
    <Message type="important">
    Your folder must be empty to be deleted. Folders containing other folders or secrets cannot be deleted.
    </Message>
2. Select the [region](/identity-and-access-management/secret-manager/concepts/#region) in which to delete the folder, in the **Region** drop-down.
3. Click the <Icon name="delete" /> icon next to the folder you want to delete.
4. Type **DELETE** to confirm and click **Delete folder**.
