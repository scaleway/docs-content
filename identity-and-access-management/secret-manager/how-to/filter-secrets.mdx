This page explains how to filter your [secrets](/identity-and-access-management/secret-manager/concepts/#secret/) using the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com).

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [generated an API key](/identity-and-access-management/iam/how-to/create-api-keys/) and enabled the `SecretManagerFullAccess` [permission set](/identity-and-access-management/iam/reference-content/permission-sets/)
  - Your have [several secrets](/identity-and-access-management/secret-manager/how-to/create-secret/)
</Message>

## Filter your secrets by tags

1. Click **Secret Manager** in the **Security and Identity** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. Your secrets display.
2. In the **Tags** drop-down box type in the tag(s) you want to filter your secrets by.
     <Lightbox src="scaleway-filter-secrets-tags.webp" alt="" />
3. Click **Apply filters**. Your secrets with the required tag(s) display.
4. Click **Reset filters** once you are done.

## Filter your secrets by region

1. Click **Secret Manager** in the **Security and Identity** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. Your secrets display.
2. In the **Region** drop-down box select the region you want to filter your secrets by.
    <Message type="important">
     - You can only select one region
     - Regions you can filter your secrets by are `AMS`, `PAR` and `WAW`
    </Message>
3. Click **Apply filters**. The secrets created in the region selected display.
     <Lightbox src="scaleway-filter-secrets-region.webp" alt="" />
4. Click **Reset filters** once you are done.

<Message type="note">
  You can also filter your secrets by tags and region simultaneously.
</Message>
