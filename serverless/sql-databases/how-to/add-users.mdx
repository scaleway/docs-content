Users can connect to a database and access its data. Each one has a different set of customizable permissions.

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />
 
<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [created a Serverless SQL Database](/serverless/sql-databases/how-to/create-a-database/)
  - You have [created a policy to manage users' permissions](serverless/sql-databases/how-to/manage-permissions/)
</Message>

## How to add an existing user to a Serverless SQL Database

1. Click **Identity and Access Management (IAM)** from the top-right of your [**Organization Dashboard**](https://console.scaleway.com/organization) in the Scaleway console. The **Users** tab of the [Identity and Access Management dashboard](https://console.scaleway.com/iam/users) displays.

2. Click the **email** of the user you want to add to the database.

3. Open the **Groups & Policies** tab.

4. Click **+ Attach a policy**. The policy attachment wizard displays.

5. Choose **Select an unassigned policy**, then, select the previously created policy from the drop-down menu.

    <Message type="note">
        You can also [create a new policy](serverless/sql-databases/how-to/manage-permissions/) or duplicate an existing one to manage the permissions of a user.
    </Message>

6. Click **Validate**.

## How to add an existing application to a Serverless SQL Database

1. Click **Identity and Access Management (IAM)** from the top-right of your [**Organization Dashboard**](https://console.scaleway.com/organization) in the Scaleway console. The **Users** tab of the [Identity and Access Management dashboard](https://console.scaleway.com/iam/users) displays.

2. Open the **Applications** tab.

2. Click the **name** of the application you want to add to the database.

3. Click the **Groups & Policies** tab.

4. Click **+ Attach a policy**. The policy attachment wizard displays.

5. Choose **Select an unassigned policy**, then, select the previously created policy from the drop-down menu.

    <Message type="note">
        You can also [create a new policy](serverless/sql-databases/how-to/manage-permissions/) or duplicate an existing one to manage the permissions of an application.
    </Message>

6. Click **Validate**.
