Web Hosting with dedicated IP is now available!
Looking to safeguard your online reputation and ensure your IP remains unaffected from shared usage?

This feature is tailored for you! [Enable your dedicated IP](/managed-services/webhosting/how-to/order-dedicated-ip/) during your hosting order or later via the console, and we will provide you with a dedicated IP linked directly to your hosting plan.
