A Scaleway Elements coupon code provides you with a discount or credits to use on our services. You can receive it as part of a promotional campaign or from your account manager. To redeem a coupon code, you need to [create a Scaleway account](/console/my-account/how-to/create-an-account/).

Coupon codes are unique and valid only for a defined period of time only.

<Message type="requirement">

  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com) 

</Message>

## How to redeem a Scaleway Elements coupon code

1. From your Organization Dashboard, click **View Billing Page** in the **Current Consumption** section. The billing pgae displays.

  <Lightbox src="scaleway-organization-dashboard.png" alt="" />

2. Scroll down to the **Discounts** section.

3. Enter the coupon code in the form, and click **Redeem coupon** to activate it.

  <Lightbox src="scaleway-redeem-coupon.png" alt="" />

  <Message type="note">

  A discount may either be a percentage of your monthly invoice or a fixed sum.

  </Message>

  Once the coupon is redeemed it displays in the list of your coupons.
