This page shows you how to activate and monitor your Scaleway products from your Cockpit using the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/).

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
</Message>

1. Click **Cockpit** in the Observability section of the [console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. The **Cockpit** overview page displays.
2. Click **Activate** to activate your Cockpit. Your Cockpit will be ready in a matter of minutes.
