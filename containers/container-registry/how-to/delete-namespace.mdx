<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com) 
  - You have created a [Container Registry namespace](/containers/container-registry/how-to/create-namespace)
</Message>

1. Click **Container Registry** in the **Containers** section of the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com) side menu. The Container Registry namespaces dashboard displays.
2. Click <Icon name="more" /> next to the namespace you want to delete, then select **Delete** from the drop-down menu. A pop-up displays. 
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-registry_delete_namespace.webp" />
3. Type **DELETE** to confirm the deletion of the namespace. Then click **Delete this namespace**.
    <Message type="tip">
      A namespace can also be deleted from the **Namespace Settings** tab, by clicking on the **Delete namespace** Button.
    </Message>

    <Message type="important">
      This action is irreversible and all data associated with this namespace will be deleted.
    </Message>
