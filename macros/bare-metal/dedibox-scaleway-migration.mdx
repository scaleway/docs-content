<Message type="note">
  You can now manage resources from both the Scaleway and the Dedibox consoles. However, ordering Dedibox servers from the Scaleway console is only possible if you have added a SEPA payment method in your **[Dedibox console](https://console.online.net)**. If you do not complete this step, you can only use the Dedibox console to place orders (see the [Dedibox documentation](/dedibox/quickstart/)).
</Message>
