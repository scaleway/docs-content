This page shows you how to manage your functions namespace. [Namespaces](/serverless/functions/concepts/#namespace) allow you to group your functions and share environment variables between them.

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [created a functions namespace](/serverless/functions/how-to/create-a-functions-namespace/)
</Message>

1. Click **Functions** in the **Serverless** section of the side menu. The functions page displays.
2. Click the functions namespace you want to manage. 
3. Click the **Namespace settings** tab. Here you can view information about the region, registry endpoint and description of your namespace.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-manage-func-namespace.webp" alt="" />
4. Add any additional environment variables you wish to add, by clicking **+Add**, entering the key/value pair, and clicking **Save**.
