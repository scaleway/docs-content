This page explains how to delete a [secret](/identity-and-access-management/secret-manager/concepts/#secret) using the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com).

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [generated an API key](/identity-and-access-management/iam/how-to/create-api-keys/) and enabled the `SecretManagerFullAccess` [permission set](/identity-and-access-management/iam/reference-content/permission-sets/)
  - You have [created a secret](/identity-and-access-management/secret-manager/how-to/create-secret/)
</Message>

1. Click **Secret Manager** in the **Security and Identity** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. Your secrets display.
2. Click <Icon name="more" /> next the secret you want to delete.
3. Click **Delete**. A pop-up displays.
4. Type **DELETE** and click **Delete secret**.

<Message type="important">
  Deleting a secret is a permanent action. It erases every version you have created for your secret.
</Message>
