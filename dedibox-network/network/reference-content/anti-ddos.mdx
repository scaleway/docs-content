Dedibox offers automatic anti-DDoS protection for all its dedicated servers as a standard feature free of charge.

This protection is powered by advanced detection and mitigation algorithms utilizing cutting-edge Arbor® Networks Peakflow and Sevi® M6-NG devices. By swiftly identifying potential threats, Dedibox promptly filters out malicious traffic, ensuring minimal disruption to services.

The strategically positioned equipment at the core of our network facilitates the automatic detection of DDoS attacks and effectively neutralizes any malicious activity, safeguarding the smooth operation of your services.
