  ## Audit trail

  ### Audit trail supported endpoints

    The following [Audit trail](/audit-trail/) endpoints are supported and will be logged in Audit Trail:

    - CreateExportJob (`POST /audit-trail/v1alpha1/regions/{region}/export-jobs`)
    - DeleteExportJob (`DELETE /audit-trail/v1alpha1/regions/{region}/export-jobs/{export_job_id}`)
</Accordion>
