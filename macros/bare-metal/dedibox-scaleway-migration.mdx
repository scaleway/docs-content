Only users who **have completed** the linking of their the Dedibox account to the Scaleway console are granted access to the Dedibox section within the Scaleway console. For detailed information on the migration process, please refer to our [account linking documentation](/bare-metal/dedibox/how-to/link-dedibox-account/). **If you cannot locate the Dedibox link in Scaleway console's side menu, you can use the [Dedibox console](https://console.online.net)** to place orders, manage your Dediboxes, and access the [related documentation](/dedibox/dedicated-servers/quickstart/).
