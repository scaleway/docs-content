This page shows how to reboot your [Mac mini](/bare-metal/apple-silicon/concepts/#mac-mini) from the Scaleway console. This is useful if your Mac mini is unreachable, or you want to reboot it remotely.

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [created a Mac mini](/bare-metal/apple-silicon/how-to/create-mac-mini/)
</Message>

1. Click **Apple silicon** in the **Bare Metal** section of the side menu. A list of your Mac minis displays.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-m1-list.webp" alt="" />
2. Click the Mac mini you want to reboot. Your Mac mini **Overview** displays.
3. Scroll down to the **Reboot Mac mini** section, and click **Reboot**.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-m1-reboot.webp" alt="" />
4. Click **Reboot Mac mini** to confirm.
    <Message type="important">
      Rebooting a Mac mini is similar to pulling the electrical plug on a running computer, which can cause data corruption. To avoid data loss, save your documents before rebooting the machine.
    </Message>
