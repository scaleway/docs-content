We are excited to expand the availability of our L4 Instances, our most versatile and cost-effective GPU instances ! L4 GPUs are now available in a second AZ in Paris (par-1, in addition of par-2) enabling high availability inference infrastructure for your projects. As a reminder, L4 GPU instances are also available in Warsaw region (waw-2)


Key features include:

- Nvidia L4 24 GB (Ada Lovelace architecture)
- 4th generation Tensor cores
- 4th generation RT cores (graphics capability)
- Available in multi-GPU 1, 2, 4 or 8 GPUs
