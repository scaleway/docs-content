**New Billing Space Now Available!**

We're excited to announce the launch of our new Billing Space! You can access this space from the right-hand menu of the console. Here's what you'll find:

- **Overview Page**: Get essential information for managing your usage and invoices.
- **Consumption Management**: Track your budget alerts and soon, the Cost Manager tool for cost tracking.
- **Optimization Page**: Access vouchers and upcoming cost optimization offers.
- **Billing and Payments Information**: Dedicated section for all your billing and payment details.

Explore these new features today and manage your billing more efficiently than ever before!
