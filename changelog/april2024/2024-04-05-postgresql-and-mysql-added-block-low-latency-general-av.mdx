Block Storage Low Latency comes with two flavors: 5,000 IOPS or 15,000 IOPS for the most intensive workloads.
It is only available on the DB-PLAY2, DB-PRO2 and DB-POP2 instances and in the following Availability Zones: PAR1, PAR2, AMS1, AMS3 and WAW3, for now.
