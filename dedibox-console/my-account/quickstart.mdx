[Scaleway Dedibox](https://www.scaleway.com/en/dedibox/) offers high quality dedicated servers. With over 15 years experience, our Dedibox dedicated servers are recognized for their extreme reliability and best-in-class redundancy. We manage a continuously growing park of more than 100,000 servers in our datacenters in France and the Netherlands. This quality allows us to offer up to 99.95% SLA.

## How to create a Scaleway Dedibox account

1. Open the [Scaleway Dedibox](https://www.scaleway.com/en/dedibox/) website in a web browser.
2. Click on **Sign up** in the top right corner of the website: 
    <Lightbox src="scaleway_ddx_signup.webp" alt="" />
    The sign-up form displays. 
3. Fill in all the required information, validate the terms of service, and click **Create my account**:
    <Lightbox src="scaleway_ddx_subscribe.webp"  alt="" />

    You receive an email containing a link, to finalize your account creation.
4. Click on the link to validate your email address and to activate your account. The [Dedibox console](https://console.online.net) login screen displays.  
5. Enter your chosen username and its associated password and click **Sign In**:
    <Lightbox src="scaleway_ddx_login.webp" alt="" />

You are now connected to the Scaleway Dedibox console and you can order your first dedicated server:

<Lightbox src="scaleway_ddx_console.webp" alt="" />

## How to add a payment method

You will be asked to add a payment method during the order process of your first Dedibox. If you want to add a payment method before you order a product, you can do it as follows:

1.  Click on your user name next to *logged in as:* in the top right corner of the Dedibox console. Then click **Billing** in the pop-up menu: 
    <Lightbox src="scaleway_ddx_popup.webp" alt="" />

    The billing page displays.
2. Click **Change payment mode**: 
    <Lightbox src="scaleway_ddx_paymentmode.webp" alt="" />
3. Choose your payment mode from the list and click **Update payment mode**:
    <Lightbox src="scaleway_ddx_new_paymentmode.webp" alt="" />

    <Message type="note">
      Not all payment methods may be available for your account. 
    </Message>
4. Enter the details of your new payment mode and validate.
