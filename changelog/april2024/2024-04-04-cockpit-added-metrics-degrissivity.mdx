We are pleased to announce degressive pricing on Cockpit external metrics.

We add 6 levels for degressive monthly pricing : 
- 0 to 10B metrics : 0.15€/ Million samples
- 10 to 25B metrics : 0.13€/ Million samples
- 25B to 50B metrics : 0.12€/ Million samples
- 50 to 100B metrics : 0.11€/ Million samples
- 100 to 200B metrics : 0.10€/ Million samples
- 200B and more : 0.08€/ Million samples

Pricing is applied **in its level**. So if you send 52 Billion external samples a month, you'll be billed as so :
- 10000*0,15 : 1500€
- 15000*0,13 : 1950€
- 25000*0,12 : 3000€
- 2000*0,11 : 220€

Total : 6670€ instead of 7800€
