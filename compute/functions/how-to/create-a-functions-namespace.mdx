This page shows you how to create your first functions namespace. [Namespaces](/compute/functions/concepts/#namespace) allow you to group your [functions](/compute/functions/concepts/#function) and share environment variables between them.

<Message type="requirement">

  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)

</Message>


1. Click **Functions** in the **Serverless** section of the side menu. The functions page displays.

  <Lightbox src="scaleway-functions-creation.webp" alt="" />

2. Click **Create a namespace**. The functions namespace creation wizard displays.

3. Complete the following steps in the wizard: 

    - Enter a **name** and optionally a **description** for your namespace. The name must only contain alphanumeric characters.
    - Choose a **region**, which is the geographical location in which your namespace will be deployed.
    - Enter any **environment variables** required for your namespace. Environment variables configured in a namespace will be available in all functions/apps within the same namespace. For each environment variable, click **+Add** and ender the key/value pair.

4. Click **Create a namespace** to finish.

Your namespace is now created and you are taken to its **Functions** tab.
