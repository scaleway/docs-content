This page shows you how to delete a functions namespace.

<Macro id="requirements" />

<Message type="important">
  - This action is irreversible and permanently deletes your namespace and its functions.
  - This will delete the Container Registry namespace associated with the functions namespace where all of your functions images are stored.
</Message>

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [created a functions namespace](/serverless/functions/how-to/create-a-functions-namespace/)
</Message>

1. Click **Functions** in the **Serverless** section of the side menu. The functions page displays.
2. Click the <Icon name="more" /> icon next to the namespace you want to delete, and select **Delete** from the drop-down menu.
3. Type **DELETE** in the pop-up that asks you to confirm the action, then click **Delete namespace**.

<Message type="tip">
  You can also delete a namespace from the **Namespace settings** tab.
</Message>
