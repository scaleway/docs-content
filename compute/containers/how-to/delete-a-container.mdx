This page shows you how to delete a Scaleway Elements container

<Message type="important">
  This action will permanently delete your container. This action is irreversible and all data will be destroyed.
</Message>

<Message type="requirement">

  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [created a container](/compute/containers/how-to/deploy-a-container/)

</Message>

1. Click **Containers** in the **Compute** section of the side menu. The list of your Containers displays.

2. Click the relevant Containers namespace.

2. Click the <Icon name="more" /> icon next to the container you want to delete, and select **Delete** from the drop down menu. 

3. Type **DELETE** in the pop-up that asks you to confirm the action, then click **Delete this container**.
