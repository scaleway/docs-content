import Requirements from '@macros/iam/requirements.mdx'

<Requirements />

- A Scaleway account logged into the [console](https://console.scaleway.com)
- [Owner](/iam/concepts/#owner) status or [IAM permissions](/iam/concepts/#permission) allowing you to perform actions in the intended Organization
- Created a [Data Orchestrator workflow](/data-orchestrator/how-to/create-workflow/)

<Message type="important">
  This action will permanently delete your workflow and its associated run history. If needed, ensure you have [exported your Audit Trail event logs](/audit-trail/how-to/manage-adt-export/).
</Message>

1. Click **Data Orchestrator** in the **Data & Analytics** section of the [console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. The list of your workflows displays.
2. Click the name of the workflow you want to delete. The workflow **Overview** tab displays.
4. Navigate to the **Settings** tab.
3. Click **Delete Workflow**.
4. Type **DELETE** to confirm.
5. Click **Delete Workflow**.
