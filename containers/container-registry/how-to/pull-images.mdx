After [configuring Docker on your local machine](/containers/container-registry/how-to/connect-docker-cli), you can use it to pull images from your Container Registry.

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com) 
  - You have created a [Container Registry namespace](/containers/container-registry/how-to/create-namespace)
  - You have installed [Docker](https://www.docker.com/) on your local computer
  - You are [logged into your namespace](/containers/container-registry/how-to/connect-docker-cli)
</Message>

1. Click **Container Registry** in the **Tools** section ofthe side menu. A list of your namespaces displays.
2. Click the namespace you want to pull an image from.
3. Click <Icon name="more" />, then **Pull Instructions** next to the image you want to pull. A pop-up displays.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-registry_pull_instructions.webp" />
4. Copy the command line instructions to pull the image with Docker. Then click **OK** to close the pop-up.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-registry_pull_instructions_popup.webp" />
5. Open a terminal window and paste the instructions into it to pull the image.
  <Message type="note">
    The current configuration allows you up to 20 pulls/min per image.
  </Message>
