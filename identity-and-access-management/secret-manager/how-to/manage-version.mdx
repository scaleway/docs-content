This page explains how to manage a [version](/identity-and-access-management/secret-manager/concepts/#version) of a secret using the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com).

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [generated an API key](/identity-and-access-management/iam/how-to/create-api-keys/) and enabled the `SecretManagerFullAccess` [permission set](/identity-and-access-management/iam/reference-content/permission-sets/)
  - Your have [created a secret](/identity-and-access-management/secret-manager/how-to/create-secret/)
  - You have [created one or more versions](/identity-and-access-management/secret-manager/how-to/create-version/) for your secret
</Message>

1. Click **Secret Manager** in the **Security and Identity** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu.
2. Select the relevant [region](/identity-and-access-management/secret-manager/concepts/#region), in the **Region** drop-down.
3. Access the relevant secret. Your secret's **Overview** tab displays.
4. Click the **Versions** tab. The versions of your secret display.
5. Click <Icon name="more" /> next to the version you want to manage.
6. Click **Disable** to disable one specific version of your secret.
7. Click <Icon name="more" /> next to the version you have disabled, then click **Enable** to enable it.
8. Click **Copy version's value** to copy the value of your version.
9. Click <Icon name="seeMore" /> to see the value of your version.
