This page shows you how to activate and monitor your Scaleway products from your Cockpit using the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/).

<Message type="requirement">

 You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)

</Message>

# How to activate your Cockpit

1. Log into your [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/) account.

2. Click **Cockpit** in the side menu.

3. Click **Activate** to activate your Cockpit. Your Cockpit will be ready in a matter of minutes.
