[Qwen3 Embedding 8B](https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/generative-apis/reference-content/supported-models/) is state of the art embedding model ranking 3rd on MTEB leaderboard as of November 2025 and supporting custom dimensions number (also known as [Matryoshka embeddings](https://huggingface.co/blog/matryoshka)).
[Holo2](https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/generative-apis/reference-content/supported-models/) is a frontier model built to analyze user interfaces and take actions on them. Holo2 enables browser automation as well as thick client or mobile applications interactions.
