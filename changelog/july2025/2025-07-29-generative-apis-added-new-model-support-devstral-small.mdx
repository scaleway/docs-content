[Devstral small 2505](https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/generative-apis/reference-content/supported-models/#chat-models) is now available in Preview in Generative APIs.

Devstral small is a fine-tuned version of mistral small 3.1 optimized to perform agentic coding tasks, that can be plugged [into your favorite IDE](https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/generative-apis/reference-content/integrating-generative-apis-with-popular-tools/).
