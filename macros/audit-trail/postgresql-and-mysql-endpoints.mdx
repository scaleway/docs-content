  ## PostgreSQL & MySQL

  ### PostgreSQL & MySQL supported endpoints

    The following [PostgreSQL & MySQL](/managed-databases-for-postgresql-and-mysql/) endpoints are supported and will be logged in Audit Trail:

    - CreateDatabaseBackup (`POST /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/backups`)
    - UpdateDatabaseBackup (`PATCH /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/backups/{database_backup_id}`)
    - ExportDatabaseBackup (`POST /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/backups/{database_backup_id}/export`)
    - RestoreDatabaseBackup (`POST /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/backups/{database_backup_id}/restore`)
    - DeleteDatabaseBackup (`DELETE /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/backups/{database_backup_id}`)
    - CreateInstance (`POST /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/instances`)
    - UpdateInstance (`PATCH /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/instances/{instance_id}`)
    - DeleteInstance (`DELETE /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/instances/{instance_id}`)
    - CloneInstance (`POST /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/instances/{instance_id}/clone`)
    - RenewInstanceCertificate (`POST /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/instances/{instance_id}/renew-certificate`)
    - RestartInstance (`POST /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/instances/{instance_id}/restart`)
    - UpgradeInstance (`POST /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/instances/{instance_id}/upgrade`)
    - CreateReadReplica (`POST /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/read-replicas`)
    - DeleteReadReplica (`DELETE /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/read-replicas/{read_replica_id}`)
    - CreateReadReplicaEndpoint (`POST /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/read-replicas/{read_replica_id}/endpoints`)
    - PromoteReadReplica (`POST /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/read-replicas/{read_replica_id}/promote`)
    - ResyncReadReplica (`POST /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/read-replicas/{read_replica_id}/reset`)
    - PrepareInstanceLogs (`POST /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/instances/{instance_id}/prepare-logs`)
    - PurgeInstanceLogs (`POST /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/instances/{instance_id}/purge-logs`)
    - AddInstanceSettings (`POST /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/instances/{instance_id}/settings`)
    - SetInstanceSettings (`PUT /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/instances/{instance_id}/settings`)
    - DeleteInstanceSettings (`DELETE /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/instances/{instance_id}/settings`)
    - AddInstanceACLRules (`POST /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/instances/{instance_id}/acls`)
    - SetInstanceACLRules (`PUT /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/instances/{instance_id}/acls`)
    - DeleteInstanceACLRules (`DELETE /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/instances/{instance_id}/acls`)
    - CreateUser (`POST /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/instances/{instance_id}/users`)
    - UpdateUser (`PATCH /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/instances/{instance_id}/users/{name}`)
    - DeleteUser (`DELETE /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/instances/{instance_id}/users/{name}`)
    - CreateDatabase (`POST /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/instances/{instance_id}/databases`)
    - DeleteDatabase (`DELETE /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/instances/{instance_id}/databases/{name}`)
    - SetPrivilege (`PUT /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/instances/{instance_id}/privileges`)
    - CreateSnapshot (`POST /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/instances/{instance_id}/snapshots`)
    - UpdateSnapshot (`PATCH /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/snapshots/{snapshot_id}`)
    - DeleteSnapshot (`DELETE /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/snapshots/{snapshot_id}`)
    - CreateInstanceFromSnapshot (`POST /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/snapshots/{snapshot_id}/create-instance`)
    - CreateEndpoint (`POST /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/instances/{instance_id}/endpoints`)
    - MigrateEndpoint (`POST /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/endpoints/{endpoint_id}/migrate`)
    - DeleteEndpoint (`DELETE /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/endpoints/{endpoint_id}`)
    - ApplyInstanceMaintenance (`POST /rdb/v1/regions/{region}/instances/{instance_id}/apply-maintenance`)
</Accordion>
