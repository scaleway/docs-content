What’s new in v.2?
* Connect your favorite tools, like Airflow, directly to your Apache Spark™ cluster and submit jobs effortlessly.
* Connect your Spark™ clusters to any compatible data source.
* Install custom or classic libraries (such as SparkSQL) and explore your data visually with graphs in JupyterLab.
* Easy Machine Learning with TensorFlow and MLlib.
* Monitor and analyze cluster and job performance with the Apache Spark™ UI.
* Your Spark™ cluster is now securely accessible via Private Networks.
