You can access the cPanel interface from the Scaleway console to autonomously mange your Web Hosting plan.

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [registered a domain name](/network/domains-and-dns/how-to/register-internal-domain/) at Scaleway or another registrar
  - You have a Web Hosting plan
</Message>

1. Click **Web Hosting** in the **Managed Services** section of the [console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. The **Web Hosting** overview page displays.
2. Click <Icon name="seeMore" /> or the domain name of the Web Hosting you want to configure. The **Hosting information** page displays.
3. Click **Dashboard** next to **Link to cPanel** to open the cPanel interface in a new tab.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-webhosting-cpanel.webp" alt="" />
    <Message type="tip">
        Alternatively, you can reach cPanel via the **cPanel** button in the top right corner of the **Hosting information** section.
    </Message>
4. Enter your cPanel credentials and click **Log in** to log into cPanel. 

<Message type="tip">
    For more information about cPanel, refer to [Web Hosting reference content](/managed-services/webhosting/reference-content/).
</Message>
