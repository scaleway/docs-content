This page provides information about the capabilities and limits of Scaleway Transactional Email service, depending on the quotas users have chosen.

Every Organization has quotas, which are limits on the number of Scaleway resources they can use. Below is a list of quotas available for Transactional Email.

<Message type="note">
 If you have already validated your payment method and your identity and want to increase your quota beyond the values shown on this page, [contact our support team](https://console.scaleway.com/support/create).
</Message>


|                                     | Payment method validated | Identity validated |
|-------------------------------------|--------------------------|--------------------|
| Max. of recipients                  | 3                        | 3                  |
| Domains per Organization            | 2                        | 5                  |
| Emails sent per hour                | 50                       | 200                |
| Emails sent per month               | 500                      | 5000               |
| Advanced attachments                | 0                        | 0                  |
| Max. of attachments                 | 2                        | 2                  |
| Unrestricted attachments            | 0                        | 0                  |
| Max. size of an email (Mo) for API  | 2*                       | 2*                 |
| Max. size of an email (Mo) for SMTP | 2*                       | 2*                 |

*Including the email and the attachments in the email.

**Advanced attachments** refer to specific attachments such as:

- PDF attachments
- HTML attachments
- Text attachments

<Message type="important">
  Keep in mind that when sending an email with Transactional Email, the number of recipients counts as an email. Example: If you send an email to one recipient and you add three recipients as CC, you will be billed for 4 emails.
</Message>
