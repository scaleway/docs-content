We are excited to introduce our latest versatile and cost-effective GPU Instances based on [Nvidia L4](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/data-center/l4/) in the Paris (PAR2) and Warsaw (WAW2) regions.

Key features include:
- Nvidia L4 24 GB (Ada Lovelace architecture)
- 4th generation Tensor cores
- 4th generation RT cores (graphics capability)
- Available in multi-GPU 1, 2, 4 or 8 GPUs
