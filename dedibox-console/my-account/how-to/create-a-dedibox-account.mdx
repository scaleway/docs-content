1. Open the [Scaleway Dedibox](https://www.scaleway.com/en/dedibox/) website in a web browser.
2. Click **Sign up** in the top right corner of the website:
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-dedibox-signup.webp" alt="" />
3. Fill in the required information, validate the terms of service, and click **Create my account**. A link is sent to your email address.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-dedibox-subscribe.webp" alt="" />
4. Click the link to validate your email address and to activate your account. The [Dedibox console](https://console.online.net) login screen displays.
5. Enter your chosen username and password and click **Sign In**:
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-dedibox-login.webp" alt="" />

You are now connected to the Scaleway Dedibox console and you can order your first dedicated server:

<Lightbox src="scaleway-dedibox-console.webp" alt="" />
