This page explains how to delete a [version](/identity-and-access-management/secret-manager/concepts/#version) of a secret using the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com).

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [generated an API key](/identity-and-access-management/iam/how-to/create-api-keys/) and enabled the `SecretManagerFullAccess` [permission set](/identity-and-access-management/iam/reference-content/permission-sets/)
  - Your have [created a secret](/identity-and-access-management/secret-manager/how-to/create-secret/)
  - You have [created one or more versions](/identity-and-access-management/secret-manager/how-to/create-version/) for your secret
</Message>

1. Click **Secret Manager** in the **Security and Identity** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. Your secrets display.
2. Click the secret for which you want to delete the version. Your secret's overview page displays.
3. Click the **Versions** tab.
4. Click <Icon name="more" /> next to the version you want to delete.
5. Click **Delete**. A pop-up displays.
6. Type **DELETE** and click **Delete version**.

<Message type="important">
  Deleting a version is permanent. You will not be able to use the version again.
</Message>
