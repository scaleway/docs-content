  ## InterLink

  ### InterLink supported endpoints

    The following [InterLink](/interlink/) endpoints are supported and will be logged in Audit Trail:

    - CreateLink (`POST/interlink/v1beta1/regions/{region}/links`)
    - UpdateLink (`PATCH/interlink/v1beta1/regions/{region}/links/{link_id}`)
    - DeleteLink (`DELETE/interlink/v1beta1/regions/{region}/links/{link_id}`)
    - AttachVpc (`POST/interlink/v1beta1/regions/{region}/links/{link_id}/attach-vpc`)
    - DetachVpc (`POST/interlink/v1beta1/regions/{region}/links/{link_id}/detach-vpc`)
    - AttachRoutingPolicy (`POST/interlink/v1beta1/regions/{region}/links/{link_id}/attach-routing-policy`)
    - SetRoutingPolicy (`POST/interlink/v1beta1/regions/{region}/links/{link_id}/set-routing-policy`)
    - DetachRoutingPolicy (`POST/interlink/v1beta1/regions/{region}/links/{link_id}/detach-routing-policy`)
    - EnableRoutePropagation (`POST/interlink/v1beta1/regions/{region}/links/{link_id}/enable-route-propagation`)
    - DisableRoutePropagation (`POST/interlink/v1beta1/regions/{region}/links/{link_id}/disable-route-propagation`)
    - CreateRoutingPolicy (`POST/interlink/v1beta1/regions/{region}/routing-policies`)
    - UpdateRoutingPolicy (`PATCH/interlink/v1beta1/regions/{region}/routing-policies/{routing_policy_id}`)
    - DeleteRoutingPolicy (`DELETE/interlink/v1beta1/regions/{region}/routing-policies/{routing_policy_id}`)

</Accordion>
