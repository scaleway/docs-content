What’s new in v.2?
* Seamless cluster access: Connect your favorite tools, like Airflow, directly to your Spark cluster and submit jobs effortlessly.
* Freedom with data sources: Break free from limitations! Connect your Spark clusters to any compatible data source.
* Libraries & Graphs at your fingertips: Install custom or classic libraries (i.e SparkSQL!) and explore your data visually with graphs in JupyterLab.
* ML made easier: Need TensorFlow? We’ve got you covered. Or dive into MLlib, your choice!
* Spark UI unleashed: Finally monitor and analyze cluster and job performance
* Private endpoint access: Your Spark cluster is now securely accessible via VPC private endpoints.
