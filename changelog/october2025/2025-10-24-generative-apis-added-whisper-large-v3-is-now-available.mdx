[Whisper Large v3](https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/generative-apis/reference-content/supported-models/) is now available on Generative APIs.

Both Whisper Large v3 and Voxtral Small 24B can now be queried to perform audio transcription using [Audio Transcriptions API](https://www.scaleway.com/en/developers/api/generative-apis/#path-audio-create-an-audio-transcription).
