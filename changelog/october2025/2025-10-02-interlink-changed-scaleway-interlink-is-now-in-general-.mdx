title: Scaleway InterLink is now in General Availability
status: changed
date: 2025-10-02
category: network
product: interlink
---

Scaleway InterLink in now available in General Availability for both flavors:
 - InterLink Hosted: connect you on-premise infrastructure to Scaleway VPC thanks to a mutualized third party interconnection
 - InterLink Self-Hosted: connect directly your on-premise infrastructure to Scaleway VPC with a physical dedicated connection
