This page explains how to manage your [secret](/identity-and-access-management/secret-manager/concepts/#secret/) using the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com).

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [generated an API key](/identity-and-access-management/iam/how-to/create-api-keys/) and enabled the `SecretManagerFullAccess` [permission set](/identity-and-access-management/iam/reference-content/permission-sets/)
  - Your have [created a secret](/identity-and-access-management/secret-manager/how-to/create-secret/)
</Message>

1. Click **Secret Manager** in the **Security and Identity** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. Your newly created secrets display.
2. Click <Icon name="more" /> next to the secret you want to manage.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-secret-manager-more-info.webp" alt="" />
3. Click **More info**. Your secret's overview page displays.
4. Click **Copy** to copy your secret's ID.
5. Click the **Tags** bar and add more tags to organize your secret.
6. Copy the code examples for the applications from which you want to get your secret.
