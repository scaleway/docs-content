<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com) 
  - You have performed the [linking process](/bare-metal/dedibox/how-to/link-dedibox-account/) between Dedibox and Scaleway
  - You have [ordererd](/bare-metal/dedibox/how-to/order-dedibox) a Dedibox server
  - You have [ordered](/bare-metal/dedibox/how-to/order-failover-ip/) a failover IP
</Message>

The following pages provide information how to configure a [failover IP](/bare-metal/dedibox/concepts/#failover-ips) on Dedibox servers running various operating systems:

* [CentOS](/bare-metal/network/reference-content/configure-centos/)
* [Debian and Ubuntu Linux](/bare-metal/network/reference-content/configure-debian-ubuntu/)
* [FreeBSD](/bare-metal/network/reference-content/configure-freebsd/)
* [Windows Server](/bare-metal/network/reference-content/configure-windows/)

Further information about failover IPs refer to our dedicated [failover IP documentation](/bare-metal/dedibox/how-to/configure-failover-ip/).
