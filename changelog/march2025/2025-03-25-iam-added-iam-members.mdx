IAM members can now be created, managed and deleted in Scaleway Organizations. 

Members are users that only exist in one Organization and that cannot not own their own Organization. IAM Administrators can manage members credentials and enforce security requirements to members for improved Organization security. Guest creation remain possible but will be deprecated in the following months.

Read our [documentation on members](https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/iam/how-to/manage-members) to learn more.
