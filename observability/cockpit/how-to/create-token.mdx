This page shows you how to create [tokens](/observability/cockpit/concepts/#tokens), to grant other cloud providersâ€™ services and platforms access to your [Cockpit](/observability/cockpit/concepts/#cockpit).

<Message type="requirement">

 - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
 - You have activated your [Cockpit](/observability/cockpit/how-to/activate-cockpit)

</Message>

1. Log into your [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com/) account.

2. Click **Cockpit** in the side menu. The **Cockpit** overview page displays.

3. Click the **Tokens** tab.

4. Click **Generate new token** to create a token. A pop-up displays prompting you to create a token.

5. Choose a name for your token or use the already-generated name.

6. Select the permissions you wish to apply to your token.

7. Click **Generate token** to generate your token. A pop-up displays with your secret key. Make sure to save your secret key before closing the pop-up.

You newly-created token displays in the list of your tokens.
