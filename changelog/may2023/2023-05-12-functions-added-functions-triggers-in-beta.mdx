Functions can now be triggered from Message Queues hosted in Messaging and Queuing SQS namespaces.
Simply add an [SQS Trigger](/serverless/functions/how-to/add-trigger-to-a-function/) from the console and when a new message is sent to the queue, it will trigger your function.
