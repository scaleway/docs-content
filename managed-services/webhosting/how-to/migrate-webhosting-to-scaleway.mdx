You can now migrate your Dedibox Web Hosting Cloud (powered by cPanel) to our new web hosting service in the Scaleway ecosystem. Providing a user-friendly graphical interface for optimizing your web hosting management. By migrating to this new and improved platform, you will enjoy all the features included in your existing Dedibox **Web Hosting Cloud** package, along with the most recent updates and enhanced performance enhancements.

<Message type="important">
    Currently, the migration is only possible for users having a [Web Hosting Cloud (cPanel) plan](/dedibox-console/cpanel-hosting/quickstart/) in the Dedibox console. Users having a Web Hosting Classic plan will be able to migrate at a later point.
</Message>

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have a [Web Hosting Cloud (cPanel)](/dedibox-console/cpanel-hosting/quickstart/) plan in the Dedibox console
</Message>

1. Go to the [list of web hosting accounts](https://console.online.net/fr/mut/list) and select the web hosting you want to migrte.
2. Click **Migrate** on the webhosting overwiew page. A pop-up displays.
3. Review the information regarding your web hosting and click **Migrate** to pre-fill the migration information in the Scaleway console. You are being redirected to the Scaleway console.
4. The migration information for your webhosting display. Verify that you are in the [destination project](/console/my-project/quickstart/) for your hosting. Then click **Migrate your Web Hosting**.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-webhosting-migrate-console.webp" alt="" />
    <Message type="important">
        Please be aware that the migration process will be completed within a span of two business days.
    </Message>
