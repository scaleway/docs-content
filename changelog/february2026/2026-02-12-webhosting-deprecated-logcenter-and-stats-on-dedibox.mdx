As part of ongoing infrastructure improvements, the Logcenter and Stats services on Dedibox are being deprecated.

The impacted endpoints are logcenter.online.net and stats.online.net.

If you are currently using these services, we invite you to migrate your hosting to Scaleway to continue benefiting from logging and statistics features in our updated environment.
