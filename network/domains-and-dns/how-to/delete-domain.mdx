<Macro id="iam-requirements" />
 
<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com) 
  - You have [linked your domain](/network/domains-and-dns/how-to/add-external-domain) with Scaleway DNS
</Message>

1. Click **DNS** in the **Network** section of the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com) side menu. The DNS zone dashboard displays.
2. Click <Icon name="more" />, then **More info** next to the domain you want to delete.
3. Click **Delete domain**. A pop-up displays. 
4. Confirm the deletion of the domain by typing **DELETE**, then click **Delete this domain**.  
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-dns-delete-domain.webp" alt="" />
    <Message type="important">
      This will permanently delete your external domain and its related zones from Scaleway DNS. This action is irreversible and your domain name may become unresolvable. 
    </Message>

  <Message type="tip">
    You can also delete your domain from the list of domains by clicking <Icon name="more" />, then **Delete**.
  </Message>
