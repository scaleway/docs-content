The [image privacy policy](/containers/container-registry/concepts/#image-privacy-policies) specifies whether everyone is granted access to pull the image or not.

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com) 
  - You have created a [Container Registry namespace](/containers/container-registry/how-to/create-namespace)
  - You have [pushed an image](/containers/container-registry/how-to/push-images) to your namespace 
</Message>

1. Click **Container Registry** in the **Containers** section of the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com) side menu. The Container Registry namespaces dashboard displays.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-registry_namespace_list.webp" />
2. Click the namespace's name to list its images.
3. Click <Icon name="more" /> next to the container image name, then **Privacy policy**.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-registry_image_privacy.webp" />
4. Select the new image privacy policy to apply. The policy can either be **Public**, **Private** or **Inheritance from namespace policy**.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-registry_privacy_policy.webp" />
5. Click **Update privacy policy** to set the policy.
