Starting September 1st 2023, Kapsule and Kosmos clusters are limited to 150 nodes maximum on first offer. However, to go beyond 150 nodes, Scaleway offers a range of dedicated control planes to cater to a wide range of use-cases. Refer to our [Kubernetes control plane offers overview](/containers/kubernetes/reference-content/kubernetes-control-plane-offers/) for more information.
