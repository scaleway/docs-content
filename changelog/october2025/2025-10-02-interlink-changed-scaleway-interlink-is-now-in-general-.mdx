---
title: Scaleway InterLink is now in General Availability
status: changed
date: 2025-10-02
category: network
product: interlink
---

Scaleway InterLink is now in General Availability for both InterLink types:
 - InterLink Hosted: connect your on-premises infrastructure to your Scaleway VPC via a partner, with a mutualized third-party interconnection
 - InterLink Self-Hosted: directly connect your on-premises infrastructure to your Scaleway VPC with your own physical dedicated connection
