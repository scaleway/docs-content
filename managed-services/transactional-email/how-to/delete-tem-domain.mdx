This page shows you how to delete your domain.

<Message type="requirement">
 - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
 - You have [configured your domain name with Transactional Email](/managed-services/transactional-email/how-to/configure-domain-with-transactional-email/)
</Message>

1. Click **Transactional Email** in the Network section of the [console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. The list of your domains displays.
2. Click the domain you want to delete. The overview of your domain displays.
3. Scroll down the page and click **Delete domain**.
  <Message type="important">
   This action will permanently delete your domain. Emails sent from this domain will be rejected and all the domainâ€™s information will be deleted, including activity and metrics.
  </Message>
4. Type **DELETE** to confirm.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway_delete_tem_domain.webp" alt="" />
5. Click **Delete this domain** to delete your domain.
