<Macro id="webhosting-online" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Dedibox Console](https://console.online.net)
  - You have created a **Webhosting Classic**
</Message>

<Message type="important">
  Keep in mind that the deletion of an email account is irreversible and that all emails stored on the servers will be deleted.
</Message>


1. Click **Hosting** then click **Manage**.
2. Scroll down to **E-MAIL ACCOUNTS** and click **Manage**. A list of your email accounts displays.
3. Click the **cogwheel** next to the email account you wish to delete. 
4. Click **Delete**. 
    <Lightbox src="scaleway_delete_email_account.webp" alt="" />
5. Click **Delete** to confirm the deletion.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway_confirm_deletion.webp" alt="" />


<Message type="note">
  The email account is now scheduled to be terminated within the hour. 
</Message>
