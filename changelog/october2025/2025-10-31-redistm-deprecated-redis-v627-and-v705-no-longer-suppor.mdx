---
title: Redis v6.2.7 & v7.0.5 no longer supported
status: deprecated
date: 2025-10-31
category: databases
product: redistm
---

Redis v6.2.7 & v7.0.5 will be deprecated on November 24th. Follow [this documentation](https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/managed-databases-for-redis/api-cli/upgrade-redis-version/) to upgrade to v7.2.11. Upgrades will be forced on that date if not performed before.
