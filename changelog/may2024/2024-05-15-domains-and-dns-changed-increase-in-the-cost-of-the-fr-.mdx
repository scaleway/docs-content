As you may see, the AFNIC announced that the .FR extension will increase its price by 11,1% on 1st March 2024 : https://www.afnic.fr/observatoire-ressources/actualites/le-fr-passe-de-4-56e-a-507e-a-partir-du-1er-mars-2024/

This augmentation will also impact the cost of .FR domains you buy from Scaleway, starting June 1st 2024.
