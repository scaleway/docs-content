<Macro id="requirements" />

- A Scaleway account logged into the [console](https://console.scaleway.com)
- Scaleway Partner status
- [Added a client](/partners/partner-space/how-to/add-client/)

1. Go to the Partner Space. A list of your clients displays. 
2. Locate the row corresponding to the client whose Organization you want to unlock.  
3. Click <Icon name="more" /> at the end of the row.
4. Click **Unlock Organization**. A pop-up displays.
5. Type **UNLOCK** in the text area.
6. Click **Unlock** to confirm.
