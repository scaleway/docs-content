<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
    - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/)
</Message>

1. Click **IPFS Naming** in the **Labs** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com) side menu. The IPFS Naming section displays.
2. Click **Create a Key** to create a [key](/labs/ipfs/concepts/#volume), which is the storage area for a set of pins.
<Lightbox src="scaleway-create-volume-ipfs.webp" alt="" />
3. Choose a region for the keys.
<Lightbox src="choose-region-ipfs.webp" alt="" />
4. Enter a name for the keys.
<Message type="important">
    Volume names can only contain alphanumeric characters and dashes.
</Message>

<Lightbox src="scaleway-choose-name-ipfs.webp" alt="" />
5. Click **Create a Volume**.
