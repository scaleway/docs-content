Domain names are locked by default to avoid any unsolicited outgoing transfer. In case you want to transfer your domain name to another registrar, you can unlock it from your Scaleway console. 

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com) 
  - You have a domain name registered with Scaleway Domains and DNS
</Message>

## How to unlock a domain name

1. Click **Domains and DNS** in the **Network** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com) side menu. The Domains and DNS dashboard displays.
2. Click the domain name you want to configure.
3. Click **Unlock** under **Transfer** in the domain information section. A pop-up displays.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-domain-unlock.webp" alt="" />
4. Click **Unlock** to unlock your domain name. A pop-up displays asking you to confirm.
5. Click **Unlock** again.

The operation might take a few minutes to complete.


## How to lock a domain name

1. Click **Domains and DNS** in the **Network** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com) side menu. The Domains and DNS dashboard displays.
2. Click the domain name you want to configure.
3. Click **Lock** under **Transfer** in the domain information section. A pop-up displays asking you to confirm.
4. Click **Lock** to confirm. Your domain will be locked in a matter of minutes.
