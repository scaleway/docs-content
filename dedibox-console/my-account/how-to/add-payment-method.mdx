---
title: How to add a payment method
description: This page explains how to add a payment method to your Dedibox console.

dates:
  validation: 2021-07-13
  posted: 2020-03-12
---

<Message type="requirement">

  - You have an account and are logged into the [Dedibox Console](https://console.online.net) 

</Message>

1. Click your username next to _logged in as:_ in the top right corner of the console. Then click **Billing** in the pop-up menu.

    <Lightbox src="scaleway-dedibox-popup.png" alt="" />

   The billing page displays.

2. Click **Change payment mode**.

    <Lightbox src="scaleway-dedibox-paymentmode.png" alt="" />

3. Choose your payment mode from the list and click **Update payment mode**.

    <Lightbox src="scaleway-dedibox-new-paymentmode.png" alt="" />

    <Message type="note">

    Not all payment methods may be available for your account.

    </Message>

4. Enter the details of your new payment mode and validate.
