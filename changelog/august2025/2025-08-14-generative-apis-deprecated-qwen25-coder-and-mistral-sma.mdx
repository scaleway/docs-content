[Qwen2.5 Coder](https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/generative-apis/reference-content/supported-models/) and [Mistral Small 3.1](https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/generative-apis/reference-content/supported-models/) are now deprecated in favor of [Qwen3 Coder](https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/generative-apis/reference-content/supported-models/) and [Mistral Small 3.2](https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/generative-apis/reference-content/supported-models/).

Following our [model lifecycle policy](https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/generative-apis/reference-content/model-lifecycle/), both models will remain accessible up to November 14th 2025. After this date, request will be routed automatically their most recent version.
