You can now configure the activity report's delivery with a customizable monthly or weekly frequency. Additionally, you can enable or disable the report as needed either through the [console](https://console.scaleway.com/transactional-email/settings) or [via the API](https://www.scaleway.com/en/developers/api/transactional-email/#path-project-settings-update-project-settings).
