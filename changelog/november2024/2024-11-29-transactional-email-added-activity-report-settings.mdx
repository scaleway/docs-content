You can now customize your activity report to a monthly or weekly frequency. Additionally, you can enable or disable the report as needed either through the [Scaleway console](https://account.scaleway.com/?service=elementstransactional-email/settings) or [the API](https://www.scaleway.com/en/developers/api/transactional-email/#path-project-settings-update-project-settings).
