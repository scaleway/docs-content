This page shows you how to delete a Scaleway Elements Functions Namespace.

<Message type="important">
  This action will permanently delete your Namespace and all your Functions will be lost. This action is irreversible.
</Message>

<Message type="requirement">

  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [created a Functions namespace](/compute/functions/how-to/create-a-function/)

</Message>

1. Click **Functions** in the **Compute** section of the side menu. The Functions page displays.

2. Click the <Icon name="more" /> icon next to the namespace you want to delete, and select **Delete** from the drop down menu. 

3. Type **DELETE** in the pop-up that asks you to confirm the action, then click **Delete this Namespace**.

<Message type="tip">

  You can also delete a namespace from the [Namespace settings tab](/compute/functions/how-to/manage-a-functions-namespace/).

</Message>
