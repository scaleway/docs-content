This page shows how to delete a [Messaging and Queuing namespace](/serverless/messaging/concepts/#namespace).

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [created a Messaging and Queuing namespace](/serverless/messaging/how-to/create-namespace/)
</Message>

## How to delete a namespace

1. Click **Messaging** in the **Serverless** section of the side menu. The list of your Messaging and Queuing namespaces displays.
2. Click the <Icon name="more" /> button next to the namespace you want to update. A menu displays.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-namespace-menu.webp" alt="" />
3. Select **Delete**. A popup displays.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-delete-namespace.webp" alt="" />

  <Message type="important">
    Deleting a namespace is irreversable. Any URLs, credentials and queued messages belonging to this namespace will also be deleted.
  </Message>
4. Type **DELETE** to confirm, and click **Delete namespace**.

    Your namespace is deleted. You are returned to your Messaging and Queuing overview page.
