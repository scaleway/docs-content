Take control of delivery errors with email flags. Whether it's a Hard bounce, Soft bounce, Mailbox full, or other issues, these flags provide immediate insights into the reasons behind delivery failures.

You can filter on these flags via the API and the Activity Console tab.

List here https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/managed-services/transactional-email/concepts/#flags
