You can now transition and expire non-current versions of objects in the Scaleway console with automated lifecycle rules. Refer to the dedicated documentation to fine-tune the lifecycle of your objects: https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/object-storage/how-to/manage-lifecycle-rules/
