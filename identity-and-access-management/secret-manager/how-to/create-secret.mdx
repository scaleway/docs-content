This page explains how to create a [secret](/identity-and-access-management/secret-manager/concepts/#secret) using the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com).

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [generated an API key](/identity-and-access-management/iam/how-to/create-api-keys/) and enabled the `SecretManagerFullAccess` [permission set](/identity-and-access-management/iam/reference-content/permission-sets/)
</Message>

1. Click **Secret Manager** in the **Security and Identity** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. The **Secret Manager** overview page displays.
2. Click **Create secret**.
   <Message type="note">
    The **Certificate** secret type ensures that the content of your secret's versions complies with the [PEM file](/identity-and-access-management/secret-manager/concepts/#pem) format.
   </Message>
3. Configure your secret:
    * Select the region in which you want to create your secret. Your secret will be duplicated in all the Availability Zones of the selected region.
    * Choose the [path](/identity-and-access-management/secret-manager/concepts/#path) in which you want to store your secret, or create a new path.
    * Enter a name for your secret and optional tags.
    * Add your secret manually or click <Icon name="plus" /> to import it from a file.
    <Message type="note">
     The maximum file size for your secret is 64 KiB.
    </Message>
4. Click **Create secret**.
