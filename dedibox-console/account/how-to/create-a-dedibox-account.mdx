Follow these steps to get started with Scaleway Dedibox:

1. Go to the [Scaleway Dedibox](https://console.online.net/en/user/subscribe/) website and click **Subscribe**.
2. Fill up and review the subscription form, and accept the terms of service. Then, click **Create my account**.
3. On the inbox of the email used for registration, click the verification link to activate your account. This will lead you to the [Dedibox console](https://console.online.net/en/login/) login page.
4. Enter the username and password created during registration, then click **Sign in**.

   You are now connected to the Scaleway Dedibox console and can [order your first dedicated server](/dedibox/dedicated-servers/how-to/order-a-server/).
