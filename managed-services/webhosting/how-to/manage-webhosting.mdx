You can access the cPanel interface from the Scaleway console to autonomously manage your Web Hosting plan.

<Macro id="requirements" />

- A Scaleway account logged into the [console](https://console.scaleway.com)
- [Owner](/identity-and-access-management/iam/concepts/#owner) status or [IAM permissions](/identity-and-access-management/iam/concepts/#permission) allowing you to perform actions in the intended Organization
- [Registered a domain name](/network/domains-and-dns/how-to/register-internal-domain/) at Scaleway or another registrar
- A Web Hosting plan

1. Click **Web Hosting** in the **Managed Services** section of the [console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. The **Web Hosting** overview page displays.
2. Click <Icon name="seeMore" /> or the domain name of the Web Hosting you want to configure. The **Hosting information** page displays.
3. Click **Dashboard** next to **Link to cPanel** to open the cPanel interface in a new tab.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-webhosting-cpanel.webp" alt="" />
    <Message type="tip">
        Alternatively, you can reach cPanel via the **cPanel** button in the top right corner of the **Hosting information** section.
    </Message>
4. Enter your cPanel credentials and click **Log in** to log into cPanel.

<Message type="tip">
    For more information about cPanel, refer to [Web Hosting additional content](/managed-services/webhosting/reference-content/).
</Message>
