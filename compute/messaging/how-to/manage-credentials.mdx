This page shows how to manage [credentials](/compute/messaging/concepts#namespace) for a [Messaging namespace](/compute/messaging/concepts#namespace). Credentials for an SQS/SNS namespace can be updated to change their permissions, or revoked. Credentials for a NATS namespace can only be revoked, not updated. Once credentials are revoked, any user trying to use the credentials to connect to the Messaging namespace will be denied.

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [created a Messaging namespace](/compute/messaging/how-to/create-namespace/)
  - You have [created credentials](/compute/messaging/how-to/create-credentials/)
</Message>

## How to update SQS/SNS credentials

1. Click **Messaging** in the **Serverless** section of the side menu. The list of your Messaging namespaces displays.
2. Click the SQS/SNS namespace whose credentials you want to update. The namespace's **Overview** page displays.
3. In the **Credentials** panel, click the <Icon name="more" /> button next to the credentials you want to update. A menu displays.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-update-credentials.webp" alt="" />
4. Select **Update credentials**. A popup displays.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-update-credentials-popup.webp" alt="" />
5. Make the required updates to your credentials. You can modify the name, and the permissions.
6. Click **Apply new configuration**.

    The credentials are updated and you are returned to the namespace's **Overview** page. The updated credentials can be seen in the **Credentials** tab.


## How to revoke credentials

1. Click **Messaging** in the **Serverless** section of the side menu. The list of your Messaging namespaces displays.
2. Click the namespace you wish to revoke credentials for. The namespace's **Overview** page displays.
3. In the **Credentials** panel, find the credentials you wish to revoke.
    - For NATS credentials, click the <Icon name="delete" /> button next to the relevant credentials to revoke them
    - For SQS/SNS credentials, click the <Icon name="more" /> button next to the relevant credentials, and select **Revoke** from the drop-down menu.

    A pop-up displays reminding you that this action is irreversible. 
4. Click **Revoke credential** to confirm. 

    The credentials are revoked and you are returned to your namespace's overview page.
