In this tutorial, you will learn how to manually configure your domain DNS records to work with your Mailbox.

<Requirements />

- A Scaleway account logged into the [console](https://console.scaleway.com)
- [Owner](/iam/concepts/#owner) status or [IAM permissions](/iam/concepts/#permission) allowing you to perform actions in the intended Organization
- [Created your Mailbox domain](/mailbox/how-to/create-mailbox-domain/)
- A domain name

## Configure your email client

1. Click **Mailbox** in the **Domains & Web Hosting** section of the [console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. The list of your domains displays.
2. Click a Mailbox domain to configure.
3. In the **DNS Records** tab, check and copy records related to email delivery, email authentication, mail client configuration, calendar, and contacts services.
You will need all these values in the next steps, so be sure to store them safely.

<Message type="important">
  The records for MX, DMARC, DKIM, and SPF are required for your domain to authenticate with your Mailbox.
</Message>

### Configure MX record

1. On your Mailbox records page, copy the **value** corresponding to the `mx` record.
2. Go to the records settings on your domain (refer to your domain manager for more information).
3. Add or set an `mx` record:
    - Record type: `MX`
    - Name: same as your domain
    - Value: Paste the **value** you have copied from the `mx` record of your Mailbox, with or without the `10` depending on your domain manager.
    - Priority: `10`
4. Save your changes.
5. On your Mailbox records page, click **Check status** to synchronize the changes with your Mailbox.

### Configure DMARC record

1. On your Mailbox records page, copy the **name** and **value** corresponding to the `dmarc` record.
2. On another tab or page, go to the records settings on your domain (refer to your domain manager for more information).
3. Add or set a `dmarc` record:
    - Record type: `TXT`
    - Name: Paste the **name** you have copied from the `dmarc` record of your Mailbox.
    - Value: Paste the **value** you have copied from the `dmarc` record of your Mailbox.
4. Save your changes.
5. On your Mailbox records page, click **Check status** to synchronize the information with your Mailbox.

### Configure other records

The other DNS records listed on the Mailbox domain are recommended or optional. 
1. On your Mailbox records page, check the information of the DNS record you wish to add to your domain.
2. On another tab or page, go to the records settings on your domain (refer to your domain manager for more information).
3. For any record that you want to add::
    - Record: Paste the **record** you have copied from the corresponding record of your Mailbox.
    - Record type: Paste the **type** you have copied from the corresponding record of your Mailbox.
    - Name: Paste the **name** you have copied from the corresponding record of your Mailbox.
    - Value: Paste the **value** you have copied from the corresponding record of your Mailbox.
4. Save your changes.
5. On your Mailbox records page, click **Check status** to synchronize the changes with your Mailbox.
