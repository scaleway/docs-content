This page shows you how to access your [managed dashboards](/observability/cockpit/concepts/#managed-dashboards) using the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/).

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com/)
  - You have [activated your Cockpit](/observability/cockpit/how-to/activate-cockpit/)
  - You have [retrieved your Grafana credentials](/observability/cockpit/how-to/retrieve-grafana-credentials/)
</Message>


1. Click **Cockpit** in the Observability section of the [console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. The **Cockpit** overview page displays.
2. Click **Open your dashboard** to open your Grafana managed dashboard. You are redirected to the Grafana website.
3. Enter your [Grafana credentials](/observability/cockpit/how-to/retrieve-grafana-credentials/).
4. Click **Log in**. The Grafana overview page displays.
5. Hover over **Dashboards** and click **Browse**.
    <Lightbox src="scaleway_grafana_browse.webp" alt="" />
6. Click the folder icon. The Scaleway folder displays.
7. Click the Scaleway folder to display Scaleway's managed dashboards.

<Message type="important">
 The managed dashboards in the “Scaleway” folder are always read-only.
</Message>
