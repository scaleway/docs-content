Abuse tickets are complaints regarding the hosting of copyrighted content or engaging in illegal activities using Scaleway services.

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Dedibox console](https://console.online.net) 
</Message>


1. Click your username, located next to "logged in as" at the top right corner of the Dedibox console. Then, select **Abuses** from the pop-up menu that appears.
2. A list of your abuse tickets displays. Click **Details** to view the specifics of the complaint.
3. Take note of the details provided and proceed with the necessary steps to address and resolve the issue. If you need guidance on resolving outgoing DDoS attacks from your server, refer to our documentation [how to prevent outgoing DDoS](/compute/instances/reference-content/preventing-outgoing-ddos/) for assistance.
4. After implementing the appropriate measures, respond to the author of the ticket with your actions taken and the resolution. To indicate that the ticket has been addressed, click **Resolve** to mark it as closed.
    <Message type="note">
      Refer to [our abuse notice](https://www.scaleway.com/en/abuse-notice/) for further information about Scaleway's abuse policy.
    </Message>
