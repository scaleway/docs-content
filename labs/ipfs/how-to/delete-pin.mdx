<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com/)
  - You have [created](/storage/block/how-to/create-a-volume/) a Block Storage volume
</Message>

1. Click **IPFS Pinning** in the **Labs** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com) side menu. The IPFS Pinning section displays a list of your volumes.
2. Click the <Icon name="more" /> icon next to the volume containing the pin you wish to delete, and select **Pin** from the drop-down menu. The pin section displays.
3. Click the <Icon name="more" /> icon next to the pin you wish to delete. The three IPFS gateways display along with a **Delete** button.
4. Click **Delete**. A pop-up displays.
5. Type `DELETE` to confirm.
    <Message type="important">
        This action is irreversible and will permanently delete the pin and all its associated pins.
    </Message>
6. Click **Delete Pin**. You are returned to the list of your pins, where the pin you deleted no longer appears.
