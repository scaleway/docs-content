<Message type="requirement">

- You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com) 
- You have [linked your domain](/network/dns-cloud/how-to/add-external-domain) with Scaleway Domains and DNS

</Message>

1. Click **Domains and DNS** in the **Network** section of the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com) side menu. 

The Domains and DNS dashboard displays.

2. Click <Icon name="more" />, then **More info** next to the domain you want to delete.

3. Click **Delete domain**. 
 
 A pop-up displays. 

4. Type **DELETE** to confirm the deletion of the domain, then click **Delete this domain**.  
    <Lightbox src="scaleway-dns-delete-domain.webp" alt="" />
    <Message type="important">

    This will permanently delete your external domain and its related zones from Scaleway Domains and DNS. This action is irreversible and your domain name may become unresolvable. 

    </Message>

  <Message type="tip">

  You can also delete your domain from the list of domains by clicking <Icon name="more" />, then **Delete**.

  </Message>
