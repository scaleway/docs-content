This page shows you how to deactivate your Cockpit.

<Message type="important">

 By deactivating your Cockpit you will no longer be able to monitor your Scaleway resources, configure the sending of external metrics and logs, or receive alerts. All associated data will be permanently deleted.

</Message>


<Message type="requirement">

  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com/)
  - You have [activated your Cockpit](/observability/cockpit/how-to/activate-cockpit/)

</Message>

1. Log into your [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com/) account.

2. Click **Cockpit** in the side menu. The **Cockpit** overview page displays.

3. Click the **Settings** tab.

4. Click **Deactivate Cockpit**.

5. Type **DEACTIVATE** and then click **Deactivate Cockpit** to confirm. Your Cockpit will be deleted in a matter of minutes.

<Message type="note">

 You can reactivate your Cockpit at any time.

</Message>
