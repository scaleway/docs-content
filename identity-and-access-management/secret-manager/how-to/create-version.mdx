This page explains how to create a version of a secret using the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com).

<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com)
  - You have [generated an API key](/identity-and-access-management/iam/how-to/create-api-keys/) and enabled the `SecretManagerFullAccess` [permission set](/identity-and-access-management/iam/reference-content/permission-sets/)
  - Your have [created a secret](/identity-and-access-management/secret-manager/how-to/create-secret/)
</Message>

1. Click **Secret Manager** in the **Security and Identity** section of the [Scaleway console](https://console.scaleway.com/) side menu. Your secrets display.
2. Click the secret you want to add a version to. Your secret's overview page displays.
3. Click the **Versions** tab.
4. Click <Icon name="plus" /> and **Create a new version**. A pop-up displays.
5. Add your version manually or click <Icon name="plus" /> to import it from a file.
6. Click <Icon name="toggle" /> to enable or disable the version.
7. Click **Add new version**.
